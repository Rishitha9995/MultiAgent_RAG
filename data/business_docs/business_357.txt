Create a list of five things that a student should avoid in order to succeed in academia.
1. Procrastination: Delaying your work until the last minute can lead to compromised quality, high stress, and lower grades.
2. Skipping classes: Missing lectures or tutorials can lead to gaps in knowledge and understanding of the material, and can affect your performance on exams and assignments.
3. Poor time management: Neglecting to balance your time effectively between academic and non-academic commitments can leave you feeling overwhelmed and disorganized, resulting in a dip in productivity.
4. Not seeking help: Not asking for help or clarification when needed can cause you to fall behind or miss important concepts, sacrificing your own academic success.
5. Cheating: Engaging in dishonest academic behavior can have severe consequences, including damage to your academic reputation, expulsion, or legal ramifications. It's important to uphold academic integrity and do your work honestly.
