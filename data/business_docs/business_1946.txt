Summarize the article "How the Zero Waste Movement is Gaining Momentum".
The Zero Waste movement is steadily gaining popularity as more people become aware of the impact human consumption and waste generation has on the environment. The movement encourages reducing consumption, recycling, reusing and composting as much as possible to reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills. Many cities have implemented zero-waste policies, with San Francisco and Seattle being notable trailblazers in the United States. The trend is also catching on with businesses, sparking the growth of zero-waste stores that sell reusable and package-free products. While some see the zero-waste lifestyle as a challenging adjustment, others view it as an opportunity for creativity and mindfulness. Overall, the Zero Waste movement is driven by a collective determination to make a positive change for the planet.
