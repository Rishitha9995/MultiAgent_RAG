Name three popular news outlets
1. CNN: Cable News Network is a highly recognized American news channel that delivers the latest news and up to date information in the US and around the world.

2. BBC: The British Broadcasting Corporation is a highly respected news organization that produces and broadcasts news content online, on radio, and on television.

3. Reuters: This international news organization is renowned for providing accurate and unbiased coverage of current events, with a strong focus on finance and business news.
