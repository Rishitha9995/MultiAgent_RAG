Name two reasons why a person might seek therapy.
1. Mental Health Issues: A person might seek therapy if they are going through emotional and psychological distress, issues such as anxiety, depression, stress, or PTSD. Therapy can help them learn skills to manage their emotions, improve their quality of life, and work through past traumas.

2. Life Transitions and Challenges: Many people seek therapy to help cope with the stress of major life changes and challenges, such as the loss of a job, a divorce, or the death of a loved one. Therapy can provide a safe space to process these events and work through the emotions that accompany them.
