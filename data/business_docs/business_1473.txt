What is an example of an AI chatbot?
An example of an AI chatbot is the customer service chatbot used by many companies and businesses. These chatbots are designed to interact with customers in a lifelike and helpful manner, answering frequently asked questions, providing information about products or services, and resolving customer concerns or issues. They use natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to customer queries, making the interaction as smooth and helpful as possible. Some well-known examples of AI chatbots include the Apple's Siri, Microsoft's Cortana, and Amazon's Alexa.
