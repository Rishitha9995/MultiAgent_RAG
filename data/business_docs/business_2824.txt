List two techniques for establishing trust with customers.
1. Transparency and Communication: One important technique for establishing trust with customers is by being transparent and keeping them informed. This includes promptly addressing their concerns, responding to their inquiries or complaints, and being open about business practices, policies, and procedures. By being transparent and maintaining clear and consistent communication, customers feel confident that their needs and expectations are being considered and addressed.

2. Reliability and Consistency: Another technique for establishing trust with customers is by providing them with reliable and consistent services or products. This means fulfilling promises, meeting or exceeding expectations, and delivering high-quality products or services consistently over time. When customers can consistently count on a business to deliver great experiences or products, they are more likely to remain loyal and trust that the business has their best interest in mind.
