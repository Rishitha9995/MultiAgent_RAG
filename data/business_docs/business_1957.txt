What are three skills that an employee should possess for a managerial job?
There are several skills that are essential for an employee who is in a managerial role, but here are three of the most important ones:

1. Leadership: A manager must be able to lead and inspire their team to achieve their goals. They need to have excellent communication skills, be able to delegate tasks effectively, and motivate their team members.

2. Decision-making: A manager must be able to make sound decisions in a timely manner. This requires them to have critical thinking skills as well as the ability to analyze data, weigh pros and cons, and determine the best course of action.

3. Adaptability: The business world is constantly changing, so managers must be able to adapt to new situations and remain calm in the face of challenges. This requires them to be flexible and resilient, and able to change their strategies when needed.
