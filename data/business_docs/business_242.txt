Name two famous examples of natural language processing.
Two famous examples of natural language processing include:

1. Siri: Siri is a virtual assistant developed by Apple that uses natural language processing to interpret and respond to voice commands. Siri is used on a variety of Apple devices and allows users to perform tasks such as setting reminders, sending text messages, and making phone calls using voice commands.

2. Google Translate: Google Translate is a free online translation tool developed by Google that uses natural language processing to translate text between languages. It supports the translation of over 100 languages and can translate text, speech, and even handwriting. Google Translate is widely used by individuals, businesses, and organizations as a way to communicate with others who speak different languages.
