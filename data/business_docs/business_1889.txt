Job Title: Chef

Job Summary: 

We are seeking an experienced and passionate chef to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating, preparing, and presenting a variety of delicious dishes to our guests. The chef will work collaboratively with other members of the kitchen team to ensure a smooth and efficient operation.

Key Responsibilities: 

• Plan and develop creative menus
• Prepare and cook dishes to high standards
• Ensure consistent food quality and presentation 
• Monitor inventory, and order and restock ingredients and supplies as needed
• Manage food cost and minimize waste
• Follow kitchen hygiene and safety guidelines
• Train, supervise, and motivate kitchen staff
• Collaborate with the management team to improve the overall guest experience 

Requirements: 

• Minimum of 5 years’ experience as a chef in a high-volume restaurant or hotel environment
• Culinary degree or equivalent experience
• Excellent cooking and plating skills 
• Strong organizational and time-management abilities
• Good communication and leadership skills
• Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
• Passion for food and creativity in the kitchen
• Knowledge of local and seasonal ingredients and culinary trends

We offer competitive compensation, benefits, and opportunities for professional growth. If you are a creative, hard-working chef looking for a new challenge, please apply with your resume and portfolio of your work.
