Give an example of a social network application.
One commonly known example of a social network application is Facebook. Facebook allows users to create personal profiles, share information, photos and videos, send messages and interact with friends, family and colleagues. The platform also provides features such as groups and pages, allowing users to connect with others based on shared interests and stay updated with the latest news and trends. Additionally, Facebook offers a marketplace feature for buying and selling items, as well as gaming and events functionality for organizing and attending events with others in your community.
