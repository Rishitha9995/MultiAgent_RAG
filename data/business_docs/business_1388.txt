Describe what an entrepreneur is.
An entrepreneur is an individual who creates, organizes, and operates a business, taking on financial risk in the hope of earning a profit. They are innovative thinkers who have the vision, passion, and drive to identify a need in the market and generate new products or services to meet that need. They are willing to take risks and overcome challenges to accomplish their goals. Entrepreneurs play a critical role in economic growth by creating jobs and driving innovation.
