Create a list of five career paths for an AI engineer
1. Machine Learning Engineer: A career in machine learning involves developing algorithms that allow systems to learn and improve on their own based on data inputs.

2. Data Scientist: A data scientist is responsible for gathering, organizing, and analyzing large complex data sets to provide insights and solutions to business problems.

3. Robotics Engineer: An AI engineer may pursue a career in robotics, building intelligent systems or robots that perform tasks usually done by humans.

4. Computer Vision Engineer: A career in computer vision involves teaching computers to interpret and understand visual information, allowing them to recognize images, videos and objects.

5. Natural Language Processing Engineer: A natural language processing (NLP) engineer works on developing specialized systems and algorithms that enable computers to understand and manipulate human languages, helping develop intelligent personal assistants or chatbots.
