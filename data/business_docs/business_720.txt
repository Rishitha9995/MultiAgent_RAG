Describe the relationship between two entities.
Apple and Google are two technology giants that both produce products and services that often compete with each other. Apple is primarily known for their hardware, such as the iPhone and MacBook, while Google is more focused on software and services such as their search engine and the Android operating system. Despite their competition, they also have a symbiotic relationship, where Google pays for its search engine to be the default on Apple's devices, and Apple's devices provide a platform for Google's services. Overall, their relationship is complex as they both compete and rely on each other to thrive in the market.
