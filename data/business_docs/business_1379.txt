Here is a road trip plan from San Francisco to Los Angeles:

Day 1: 
- Depart from San Francisco early in the morning and head towards Monterey. The journey will take approximately 2 hours.
- Stop in Monterey and visit the Monterey Bay Aquarium to see marine animals and learn about local wildlife.
- After lunch, continue to drive south towards Big Sur along scenic Highway 1. The drive will take 2-3 hours, depending on traffic.
- Stop at Pfeiffer Beach, Bixby Creek Bridge, and McWay Falls to take in the stunning views and take photos.
- Arrive at your hotel in Big Sur and spend the night.

Day 2:
- After breakfast, continue to drive south along Highway 1 past Hearst Castle towards San Luis Obispo. The journey will take approximately 3-4 hours.
- Stop in San Luis Obispo to explore the local farmers' market, Mission San Luis Obispo de Tolosa, and Bubblegum Alley.
- Continue to drive south towards Santa Barbara, which will take approximately 2 hours.
- Stop in Santa Barbara to take a stroll along the beach, visit the historic Old Mission Santa Barbara, and explore the downtown area.
- Drive to your hotel in Santa Barbara and spend the night.

Day 3:
- After breakfast, continue to drive south towards Los Angeles along Highway 101. The journey will take approximately 2 hours.
- Stop in Malibu to soak up the sun, surf some waves, and visit the Getty Villa.
- Continue to drive south towards Los Angeles, arriving in the afternoon.
- Upon arrival in Los Angeles, visit famous sites such as the Griffith Observatory, the Hollywood Walk of Fame, and the Santa Monica Pier.
- End the road trip by checking into your hotel in Los Angeles.
