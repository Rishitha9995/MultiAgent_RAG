Suggest a list of features for a voice assistant
1. Voice recognition - ability to accurately understand and process user voice commands
2. Natural language processing - ability to communicate with humans using natural and intuitive language
3. Calendar and scheduling management - plan, schedule, and keep track of daily, weekly or monthly tasks, appointments or reminders
4. Smart home automation - control smart home devices such as lighting, thermostat, or security systems
5. Text and email dictation - write and send messages using voice commands
6. Music and entertainment - play and control music, access entertainment apps, or provide updates on latest news and events
7. Personalized information - provide information such as weather updates or news based on user’s location and preferences
8. Hands-free calling - make and receive calls or send messages using voice commands without having to hold or touch the device
9. Multi-language support - ability to communicate in multiple languages
10. Integration with other apps and services - ability to connect to and control other apps or services for example food delivery or ride-hailing.
