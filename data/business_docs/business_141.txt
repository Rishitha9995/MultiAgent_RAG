Describe 3 ways in which AI can help manage inventory levels.
1. Predictive Analysis: AI can help manage inventory levels by using predictive analysis techniques, which can analyze various aspects of data such as customer demand patterns, seasonal trends, and sales data to forecast inventory requirements. This helps businesses plan and maintain the right stock levels to meet the customers’ demands, reduce excess inventory, and prevent stockouts.

2. Automated Reordering: AI can also help manage inventory levels by automating the reordering process, where the system automatically triggers a reorder of a particular item when it reaches below the minimum threshold level. This technique helps businesses maintain a healthy inventory level and can save a lot of time and effort of having to manually keep track of inventory levels and reorder products.

3. Real-time Monitoring: AI can help businesses keep track of their inventory levels in real-time, allowing them to promptly respond to any changes in customer demand. Automated monitoring can also help businesses detect irregularities or discrepancies in the inventory levels in real-time, allowing them to take appropriate action to resolve the issues before they escalate.
