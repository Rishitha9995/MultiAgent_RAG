Make an accurate analogy for the term "financial risk".
Financial risk can be likened to a tightrope walker performing high above the ground without a safety net. Just as the tightrope walker has the potential to fall and face serious consequences, a person or business taking on financial risk similarly faces the possibility of incurring significant losses. However, with skill, balance, and careful planning, both the tightrope walker and the person taking on financial risk can also succeed and reach their desired goal.
