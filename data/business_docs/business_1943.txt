Provide an example of a person using the habit of donating to charity.
Samantha is a successful businesswoman who believes in giving back to her community. For the past several years, she has made a habit of donating a portion of her income to various charities every month. She researches different organizations to see which ones align with her values and have a strong impact on the causes she cares about, such as providing education to underprivileged children or protecting the environment. Once she chooses a charity, she sets up a recurring donation to automatically contribute a certain amount each month. Over the years, her contributions have helped make a significant difference in the lives of many individuals and the health of the planet.
