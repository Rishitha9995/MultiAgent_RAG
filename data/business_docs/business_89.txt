Write a paragraph describing a meeting between two friends.
The meeting between two friends is always filled with warmth, smiles and laughter. The two friends greeted each other with open arms, eyes shining with delight from seeing each other after a while. The embrace was long and heartfelt, followed by compliments and the usual gentle teasing. The conversation started to flow naturally; catching up on the latest news and events in their lives. Their faces beamed with joy as they listened attentively to each other's stories, a clear sign of the bond between them. Throughout the meeting, the friends were fully engaged and happy, enjoying each other's company and feeling grateful for their wonderful friendship.
