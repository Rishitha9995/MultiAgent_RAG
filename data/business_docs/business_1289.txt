Name 3 tourist attractions in Tokyo.
Three popular tourist attractions in Tokyo are:
1. Tokyo Skytree: This is the tallest structure in Japan, standing at over 634 meters. It offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation deck.
2. Sensō-ji Temple: Also known as Asakusa Kannon Temple, this is one of Tokyo's oldest and most significant Buddhist temples, popular with both locals and tourists alike.
3. Tsukiji Fish Market: This is the world's largest wholesale fish and seafood market, and visitors can come early in the morning to witness the tuna auctions, or enjoy fresh sushi and sashimi at one of its many restaurants.
