Give an example of how "innovation" has contributed to the development of technology
A great example of how innovation has contributed to the development of technology is the smartphone. Before the introduction of the smartphone, mobile phones were primarily used for calling and texting. However, with the launch of the first iPhone in 2007, the world was introduced to a new type of mobile device that combined the functionality of a phone, music player, and computer all into one. This device drastically changed the way people communicate and access information, turning mobile phones into indispensable tools for everyday life. Over time, features such as touchscreens, mobile apps, and advanced cameras were added, transforming smartphones into powerful devices that allow people to do everything from ordering food to conducting business. This is just one example of how innovation can drive technology forward and improve our lives in many ways.
