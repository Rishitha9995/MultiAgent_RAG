Summarize the concept of a social network
A social network is a platform that connects individuals and communities through the use of online profiles, enabling them to interact, communicate, and share information in virtual spaces. It establishes complex relationships between people and fosters a community-based structure, where individuals can connect with friends, family, and peers, join or create groups, engage with others who share common interests or experiences. Users can interact through posting, commenting, messaging, or sharing multimedia content such as photos or videos. Social networks are primarily used for social interaction, but have also become important tools for marketing, networking, and information-sharing.
