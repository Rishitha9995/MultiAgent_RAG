Identify three benefits of eating healthy.
1. Improved Health Outcomes: Eating a healthy and balanced diet helps to reduce one's risk of developing chronic diseases such as obesity, type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and certain types of cancer. It also helps to improve overall physical health, mental well-being, and longevity.

2. Better Mood and Energy Levels: Consuming nutritious foods that are rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients can help to improve brain function, reduce symptoms of depression, and stabilize mood. Healthy eating also helps to ensure that the body has the energy and nutrients it needs to perform daily activities, reducing feelings of tiredness and fatigue.

3. Improved Weight Management: Eating a healthy diet can help to prevent weight gain and reduce one’s risk of becoming overweight or obese. Healthy foods are generally lower in calorie density, meaning that one can eat more of them without consuming too many calories. This helps to promote feelings of fullness while supporting healthy weight management.
