Suggest two alternatives for conserving energy.
1. One effective way to conserve energy is by practicing energy-efficient habits at home and in the workplace. This can involve simple changes like turning off lights and electronics when not in use, adjusting the thermostat to a more moderate temperature, using energy-saving light bulbs, and unplugging chargers when they are not in use.

2. Another alternative for conserving energy is by investing in renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind or hydroelectric power. These sources are sustainable and do not produce harmful emissions like traditional fossil fuels. More and more people are installing solar panels on their homes or businesses to harness the power of the sun to generate electricity, reducing their reliance on non-renewable energy and lowering their energy bills in the process.
