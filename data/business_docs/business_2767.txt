Come up with a list of questions you can use for interviewing social media experts
1. How do you stay up-to-date with the latest trends and updates in the social media industry?
2. Can you tell us about a successful social media campaign you managed or were a part of?
3. How do you measure the success of a social media campaign?
4. How do you handle negative feedback or comments on social media accounts?
5. How do you develop a social media strategy for a new brand or business?
6. Can you share your process for creating and curating content for social media accounts?
7. How do you balance the need for engagement and interaction with maintaining brand identity and messaging?
8. How do you approach influencer collaborations and partnerships?
9. Can you share some techniques for growing and engaging with followers on different social media platforms?
10. How do you handle a social media crisis or a potential PR issue?
