Come up with three creative solutions to reduce air pollution.
1. Promoting urban green spaces: Cities should be encouraged to invest in creating and maintaining green spaces such as parks, green roofs, and vertical gardens. Not only do plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, but they also trap dust and other pollutants from the air.

2. Implementing smarter transportation methods: This includes promoting the use of electric cars, car-pooling, and public transportation to decrease the number of vehicles on the road. Additionally, cities could expand bike lanes and create incentives for people to ride bicycles or walk, which would reduce reliance on cars and improve air quality.

3. Encouraging the use of clean energy: Governments should provide incentives for businesses and homeowners to switch to renewable energy sources such as solar or wind power. This would reduce the amount of fossil fuels being burned, which in turn would decrease the amount of pollutants being released into the air.
