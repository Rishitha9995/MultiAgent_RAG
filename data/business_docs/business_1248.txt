Describe a character who had once been successful but has since been in decline
John was once a man of great success. He had everything he thought he wanted; an executive position at a fortune 500 company, a beautiful wife, a large home in the suburbs, and a summer house in the Hamptons. He was always impeccably dressed, confident and looked up to by his peers. John was the epitome of the American dream. 

But with time, John's life began to change. He began to feel the stress of his high-pressure job and its toll on his marriage. Eventually, John was let go from his job, and his wife left him shortly thereafter. John's once opulent lifestyle became a distant memory. 

John now lives in a small apartment, and he's let himself go. He's no longer the confident man he once was, and he struggles to find purpose in his days. He spends his time lamenting about the life he once had, refusing to see the opportunities that surround him. He's lost the drive that once propelled him to such great heights. John's success is now a thing of the past.
