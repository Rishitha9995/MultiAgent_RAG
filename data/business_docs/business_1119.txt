Name two common types of friendships
Two common types of friendships are platonic friendships and supportive friendships. Platonic friendships are those in which there is no romantic or sexual attraction between the two friends. They simply enjoy each other's company, have shared interests or experiences, and provide emotional support. Supportive friendships, on the other hand, are those in which one friend provides advice, encouragement, and support to the other. This type of friendship can be particularly important during times of stress or difficult challenges.
