Create an outline for a news report about climate change.
I. Introduction
   a. Overview of what climate change is- a changing global climate due to an increase in greenhouse gases and human activity.
   b. Importance of understanding the causes and impacts of climate change.

II. Causes of Climate Change
   a. Main causes- burning of fossil fuels, deforestation, agricultural practices, and transportation.
   b. Explanation of the greenhouse effect, and how human activities contribute to it.

III. Impacts of Climate Change
   a. Effects on the environment- rise in sea levels, melting glaciers and ice caps, increased frequency and severity of weather events.
   b. Effects on biodiversity and ecosystems- changes in migration patterns, loss of habitats, increased risk of species extinction.
   c. Effects on human populations- displacement, food and water shortages, health issues, and economic impacts.

IV. Efforts to Address Climate Change
   a. National and international initiatives- Paris Agreement, Kyoto Protocol, and others.
   b. Actions being taken by governments and businesses- renewable energy, reduction of emissions, and sustainable practices.
   c. Individual actions- reducing personal carbon footprint, supporting eco-friendly practices, and spreading awareness.

V. Conclusion
   a. Recap of main points- causes and impacts of climate change, efforts being made to address it.
   b. Final thoughts- the importance of continued action and collaboration to combat climate change.
