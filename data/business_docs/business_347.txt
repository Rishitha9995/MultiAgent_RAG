Give a list of environmental issues in India
India faces several environmental issues which are impacting the nation's ecosystems and the quality of life for its citizens, some of these include:

1. Air Pollution- India has some of the worlds most polluted cities due to industries, traffic, and household emissions.

2. Water Pollution- Industrial wastes, sewage, and agricultural runoff have contaminated many of the nation’s rivers and groundwater sources. 
   
3. Climate Change- India is witnessing global warming effects such as irregular Monsoon, heatwaves, and rising coast levels. 

4. Deforestation - deforestation is a major issue due to climate change, rising population and industrialization.

5. Loss of Biodiversity - Due to habitat loss, poaching and climate change India is facing the crisis of losing its rich fauna and Flora.

6. Soil degradation- Excessive use of Fertilizers, Pesticides and improper irrigation system causing soil degradation and erosion.

7. Waste Management- Rapid urbanization has led to the increase of waste generation, littering and inadequate waste-management facilities are creating a major problem.

8. Noise Pollution- Due to high traffic, large population and urbanization there is an increase in noise pollution which is harmfull for human and animal health

These are some of the major environmental issues which India is facing, however, the Government, NGOs and individual efforts are being made to solve these problems.
