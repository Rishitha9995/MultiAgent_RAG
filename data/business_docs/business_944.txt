Come up with a creative idea for a promo campaign for a smartphone.
One creative idea for a smartphone promo campaign could be a "Switch and Save" event. The core concept is for customers to trade in their old smartphone in exchange for a discount on the purchase of the new smartphone. The event can be marketed as a way for customers to save money while upgrading to the latest technology. 

The campaign can include eye-catching visuals displaying the new smartphone alongside images of stacks of cash or piggy banks to emphasize the savings aspect. The advertisements can be placed on various channels such as social media platforms, television commercials, and billboards. 

An interactive component can also be included where customers can visit the website, enter the details of their current smartphone, and instantly receive a quote for trade-in value. Additionally, social media influencers or celebrities can be leveraged to promote the campaign by sharing their own experiences of switching their phones and the benefits of the new smartphone.

To add excitement and urgency, the campaign could be time-limited, encouraging customers to act quickly to take advantage of the offer. Overall, the "Switch and Save" promo campaign offers a unique and enticing incentive for customers to upgrade to the latest smartphone technology.
