Give an example of a situation where you would use a pie chart.
A pie chart could be used to visually represent and analyze the budget allocation for a household or a company. If someone wanted to understand how their expenses were distributed across different categories, such as housing, food, transportation, entertainment, savings, and others, they could create a pie chart with each slice representing the percentage of the overall budget that goes to each category. This would provide a clear and easily understandable picture of how the money is being spent, and can help the person make more informed decisions about their budgeting.
