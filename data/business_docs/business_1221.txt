List three techniques used to influence consumers to purchase products.
1. Advertising and Promotion: Companies extensively use marketing and advertising techniques to influence consumer behavior and promote their products. Techniques used include advertisement through TV commercials, radio ads, print ads, sponsorships, and social media promotions.

2. Sales and Discounts: Sales, discounts, and time-limited offers are another way of enticing consumers to purchase a product. By offering a product at a lower price, companies create a sense of urgency and increase the perceived value of the product, which can motivate consumers to make a purchase.

3. Product Placement: Product placement is a technique in which a company strategically places their product in TV shows, movies, and other media content to increase visibility and subtly influence consumers to purchase it. This method works by associating the product with a particular lifestyle or image that appeals to the target audience.
