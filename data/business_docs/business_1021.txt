Provide a definition for data mining.
Data mining is the process of uncovering patterns, correlations, and relationships within large data sets by using various software and analytical techniques. This information is then used to make better decisions, predict future outcomes, and improve overall systemic efficiencies. Data mining is often used in areas such as market research, business intelligence, scientific discovery, and many others. It involves the use of sophisticated algorithms and statistical methods to identify hidden patterns that would otherwise go unnoticed.
