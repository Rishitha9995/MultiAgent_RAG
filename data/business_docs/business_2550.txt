Name the five basic elements of digital marketing.
The five basic elements of digital marketing are:

1. Content Marketing: This involves creating high quality content that is relevant, informative and engaging to attract and retain audiences and ultimately, drive profitable customer action.

2. Search Engine Optimization (SEO): This is the practice of optimizing online content to improve its visibility on search engines and attract organic traffic to your website or post.

3. Social Media Marketing: This involves using social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and Instagram to connect with your target audience, boost brand awareness, increase website traffic and grow your business.

4. Email Marketing: This is the use of email to promote products or services, communicate with customers or clients, and build brand loyalty, customer retention and trust.

5. Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC): This is an online advertising model where advertisers pay each time a user clicks on their ads. This can be used to drive traffic to websites, generate leads or increase sales.
