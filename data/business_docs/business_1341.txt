Name five members of Shakespeare's Globe theatre.
The Shakespeare's Globe Theatre is renowned for hosting many of William Shakespeare's most famous works. Here are the five most frequently associated actors with the theatre during Shakespeare's time (1599 - 1642):

1. William Shakespeare - playwright, poet, actor, and shareholder in the Lord Chamberlain's Men
2. Richard Burbage - actor, shareholder, and the company's leading man
3. Augustine Phillips - actor and shareholder in the Lord Chamberlain's Men
4. William Kempe - comedy actor and original sharer of the Lord Chamberlain's Men
5. John Heminges - actor, theatre manager, and financier of the King's Men.
