List three weaknesses of this product
1. Battery Life Concerns: Despite the improved battery life in iPhone 12, some users still complain about the battery not lasting long enough, especially with prolonged use of 5G networks, which can rapidly drain the battery.

2. Lack of Accessories: The iPhone 12 does not include a charger or earphones in the box, which means that customers must purchase these accessories separately at an additional cost, which can be frustrating and inconvenient for some.

3. Price: Apple iPhone 12 is a high-end device with an accompanying high price tag, which can make it less affordable and accessible for some consumers, particularly those on a tight budget.
