Name four uses for the internet.
1. Communication: Internet allows us to communicate with others through various platforms such as email, instant messaging, video conferencing, and social media.

2. Information and research: The internet provides a vast amount of information on virtually any topic, making it an excellent resource for research, education, and learning.

3. Shopping and online Transactions: the internet allows us to buy goods and services online from the comfort of our own homes, from online marketplaces, retailers, and service providers. It also allows us to perform online transactions such as banking and bill payments.

4. Entertainment: The internet provides a multitude of entertainment options such as streaming music, movies and TV shows, online gaming, and social networking.
