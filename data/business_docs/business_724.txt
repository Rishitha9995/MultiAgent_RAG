What does the expression 'prime the pump' mean?
The expression 'prime the pump' means to stimulate or encourage an action or activity, by providing something that is necessary to get it started. It relates to the process of adding water to an old-fashioned pump in order to start the flow, and it is usually used in the context of economics and finance, where a government or organization may inject funds into an economy in order to stimulate growth or spending. In a general context, it refers to taking initial action to facilitate or encourage subsequent activity.
