Identify the stakeholders involved in the given business decision.
The stakeholders involved in this business decision to outsource the IT department may include:

1. The company's management who makes the decision.
2. Board of directors who approve the decision.
3. Employees of the IT department – their jobs may be affected.
4. Shareholders – outsourcing could affect the company's financial performance and, in turn, their returns.
5. The outsourcing partner – in charge of performing the IT services for the company.
6. Customers – IT infrastructure is key to the company's products or service delivery, and outsourcing could affect it. 
7. Vendors and suppliers – any changes in IT infrastructure could affect the company's relationship with them.
