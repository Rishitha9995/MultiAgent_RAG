Create a dialogue between two people discussing the topic of climate change.
Person 1: Hey, have you read the news about the drastic climate changes happening all over the world?

Person 2: Yeah, it’s very concerning. We keep hearing about severe weather conditions, wildfires, melting glaciers and rising sea levels.

Person 1: Really terrifying. And the truth is, human activities are contributing to it. Scientists claim that the Earth is getting warmer due to increased levels of greenhouse gases.

Person 2: Absolutely, and we need to do something about it before it’s too late. Many organizations and businesses are already taking steps to reduce their carbon footprint and countries are negotiating emission reduction targets.

Person 1: Yes, and change can start with us, with small actions like reducing energy consumption, being conscious about waste disposal, and spreading awareness about the issue.

Person 2: That’s so true. We are all responsible for protecting our planet and ensuring that future generations inherit a healthy environment.
