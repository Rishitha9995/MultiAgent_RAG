Design a product review system for a website.
A product review system allows customers to leave feedback about their experiences with products sold on a website. The system enables businesses to identify areas of improvement in their products and services while providing valuable information for potential buyers. Below is an outline of the design of the system.

1. User Account:
The review system should link to a user account system to ensure that only verified customers leave reviews. Users must sign in to their accounts to submit a review.

2. Rating System:
The system should include a rating system that allows customers to rate products on a scale, such as from one to five stars. 

3. Review Forms:
Customers will need access to review forms to leave feedback about a product. The form should include fields for a title, a detailed review description, images, and videos. The review form should also allow customers to classify whether their review is positive, neutral, or negative.

4. Moderation and Approval:
All submitted reviews must undergo moderation and approval before posting to the site. The review system should include a backend interface to moderate and approve reviews.

5. Display Reviews:
The system must display reviews on the product page. Reviews should be organized, with most recent and helpful reviews appearing first. Customers should be able to sort reviews based on their relevance, rating, or date.

6. Interaction:
The review system should allow interaction from both customers and company representatives. Users should be able to reply to or like a review while company representatives should respond to negative reviews and provide solutions.

7. Review Aggregation:
The system should aggregate the product ratings and reviews to provide an overall rating for each product on its page. The overall rating should show the average star rating and the number of reviews.

8. Reporting:
The system should include reporting features that allow businesses to analyze reviews and ratings. The review system should provide insights into areas that need improvement and highlight products that customers love.
