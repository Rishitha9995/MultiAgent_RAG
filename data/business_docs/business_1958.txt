Provide a plan to find the job of your dreams.
Here is a plan for finding your dream job as a Computer Scientist at a Silicon Valley technology company:

1. **Set clear goals and objectives:** First and foremost, have a clear understanding of what you want to achieve. For example, being a Computer Scientist at a Silicon Valley technology company is already a clear goal. You can go further and list the companies you are specifically targeting to work for.

2. **Assess your skills:** Take the time to assess your skills and see where you stand compared to the job requirements. Check the companies’ job listings to get a better understanding of the qualifications and experiences required for the position.

3. **Improve your skills:** If you believe there are areas you need to improve upon, take courses or gain experience through internships or freelance work. Keep up-to-date with the latest technologies and advancements in your field.

4. **Build your network:** Connect with people who work in the same field or at the companies you are targeting. Attend industry conferences and events to meet new people and expand your network. LinkedIn can also be a useful tool for networking and getting introduced to the right people.

5. **Optimize your resume and online presence:** Make sure your resume reflects your skills and experiences relevant to the position you are targeting. Also, ensure that your LinkedIn profile is up-to-date and represent the image you want to convey to potential recruiters.

6. **Apply to job openings and follow up:** Actively apply to job openings and keep track of the positions you've applied for. Follow up with the recruiter or hiring manager to show your interest and enthusiasm for the role.

7.  **Prepare for interviews:** Ensure that you are well prepared for the interview. Research the company, prepare for commonly asked questions and have examples ready to showcase your skills and experiences. Dress professionally, be on time and follow up with thank-you notes.

By following the above steps, you can increase your chances of finding and landing your dream job as a Computer Scientist at a Silicon Valley technology company.
