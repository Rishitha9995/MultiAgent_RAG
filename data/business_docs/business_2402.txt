Make a list of recommendations for visiting a major city
If you're planning on visiting a major city, here are some recommendations that can help you make the most out of your trip:

1. Research the city's top attractions: Look up the must-see sights, landmarks, and attractions to make sure you don't miss out on anything.

2. Explore the local cuisine: A major city can offer a diverse selection of delicious food. Take some time to research the best restaurants, street food vendors, and local delicacies.

3. Use public transportation: Most major cities have well-developed public transportation systems that make it easy to get around. Plus, it's a great way to save money and reduce your carbon footprint.

4. Visit museums and galleries: Major cities are usually filled with top-notch museums and art galleries, making it an ideal opportunity to immerse yourself in the local culture, history, and art scene.

5. Take a walking tour: Join a walking tour to explore the city on foot and learn about its history from a knowledgeable guide.

6. Discover the local nightlife: Many cities come alive at night, with popular clubs, bars, and music venues, so make sure to experience the nightlife.

7. Shop local: Visit local markets and artisan shops to find unique souvenirs, and support small businesses in the process.

8. Find green spaces: Major cities can be crowded and hectic, so take a break from the hustle and bustle by visiting a park or garden to relax and enjoy some greenery.

9. Attend a sporting event: If you're a sports fan, try to catch a local game or match, which can be a fun way to experience the city's passion and energy.

10. Be respectful and mindful of local customs: Every city has its own unique customs and etiquette, so do your research and be mindful of your behavior to avoid inadvertently offending anyone.
