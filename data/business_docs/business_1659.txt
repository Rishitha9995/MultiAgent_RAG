Create a dialogue between two people who just met at a party.
Person 1: Hey, I’m Mike.

Person 2: Hi Mike, I’m Sarah. Nice to meet you.

Person 1: Nice to meet you too. So, how did you end up at this party?

Person 2: I’m friends with the host, Lisa. We’ve known each other since college. How about you?

Person 1: Same here. Lisa and I used to work together at a marketing firm.

Person 2: Oh cool! What do you do now?

Person 1: I actually started my own business. I create custom furniture. 

Person 2: That’s awesome. What made you decide to do that?

Person 1: I’ve always been passionate about woodworking, and I wanted to turn my hobby into a career.

Person 2: That’s really brave. I’m sure it’s a lot of work, but it must be so rewarding to do something you love every day.

Person 1: Absolutely. It’s definitely a challenge, but it’s worth it. How about you, what do you do for a living?

Person 2: I’m a speech therapist. I work with kids who have speech and language difficulties.

Person 1: That sounds really fulfilling.

Person 2: It is. I love seeing the progress they make and knowing I’m making a difference in their lives.

Person 1: That’s great. It’s really nice meeting people who are passionate about what they do.

Person 2: Definitely. It’s been great talking to you, Mike.

Person 1: Likewise, Sarah. Enjoy the rest of the party.
