List 5 stocks that are performing well
Below are five stocks that, according to current data, appear to be performing well as of this year:

1. Nvidia Corp (NVDA): Nvidia Corporation is a multinational technology company that specializes in the manufacture of graphics processing units (GPUs) for the gaming and professional markets and system on a chip (SoC) units for mobile devices and the automotive market.

2. Microsoft Corp (MSFT): Microsoft Corporation is one of the largest developers and providers of software and hardware globally. They helped to establish the PC industry, and it has developed a range of products and services that have revolutionized the way businesses operate and people interact.

3. Alphabet Inc. Class A (GOOGL): Alphabet Inc. is a holding company comprising several companies, the most notable of which is Google, one of the world's largest technology companies. The company has a varied suite of products which includes search engines, online advertising platforms, mobile operating systems, and cloud computing to name a few.

4. Apple (AAPL): Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company that designs, produces, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. It is mainly known for its smartphones, tablets, computers, and smartwatches.

5. Amazon.com (AMZN): Amazon.com, Inc. is a multinational technology company that specializes in e-commerce, cloud computing, AI, and digital streaming. It's one of the world's largest online retailers and is also a major producer of consumer electronics.
