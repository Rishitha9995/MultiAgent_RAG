Name two popular tourist spots in India.
Two popular tourist spots in India are:

1. Taj Mahal: This iconic mausoleum, located in Agra, is sometimes considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal and the complex is a large and impressive display of Mughal architecture.

2. Jaipur: Also known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and a popular tourist destination because of its stunning forts, palaces and its bustling markets. Some of the top attractions are the City Palace, Amber Fort, and Hawa Mahal.
