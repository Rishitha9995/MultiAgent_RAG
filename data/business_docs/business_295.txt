Write a horror story using the following words in the story: flashlight, skeleton, darkness.
It was a dark and stormy night. The wind howled through the trees, sending shivers down my spine. I was on my way to an old, abandoned mansion that sat atop a hill. My curiosity had gotten the best of me, and I couldn't resist exploring the place that locals claimed was haunted. As I approached, I could feel my heart start to race. I pulled out my flashlight, shining it onto the dilapidated building. It was like something out of a horror movie.

I slowly made my way inside, the beam of my flashlight bouncing off the walls. The air was stale, and dust hung in the air, making me cough. The sound echoed through the empty halls, making me feel like I was being watched. I tried to shake off the feeling and continued exploring.

As I descended into the basement, the darkness seemed to close in around me. I could barely see my hand in front of my face. I shone my flashlight around, trying to find something to focus on. That's when I saw it. A skeleton, hanging from the ceiling, swaying in the breeze. I screamed, dropping my flashlight. It flickered for a moment before going out, leaving me in complete darkness.

I was alone, with nothing but the sound of my own breathing to keep me company. The skeleton seemed to loom over me, a macabre reminder of my own mortality. As I fumbled for my flashlight, my fear growing with every passing moment, I realized that I wasn't alone. I could hear something moving in the darkness, coming closer and closer. But it was too late. The mansion had claimed another victim.
