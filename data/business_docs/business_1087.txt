Provide an example of a business that can use blockchain technology to improve its operations.
A food supply chain company can use blockchain technology to improve its operations dramatically. By deploying blockchain, the company can create a transparent supply chain that logs where food items have been and what they’ve been through from farm to shelf. This presents the opportunity to increase traceability and transparency and earn the trust of customers with their food supply chain operations. Distributors, handlers, and transporters can scan, validate, and verify each step of the process on the blockchain. If anything goes awry such as food spoilage or contamination, customers can quickly spot the source of the problem, minimizing the risks associated with traditional supply chains. This technology even has the potential to create smarter, more customized journeys for individual food items, providing a digital roadmap from the farm gate to the consumer's plate and empowering the food supply chain company to turn supply chain visibility into their advantage.
