Urban Lifestyle

A city that never sleeps,
A hustle and bustle that never creeps,
Towers of concrete and steel,
A lifestyle that's always on the wheel.

Streetcars and trains, bikes and feet,
All moving to a different beat.
The sound of the city, a symphony of life,
A place of opportunity, passion and strife.

Diversity and cultures, all intertwined,
A melting pot of all humankind.
A place where dreams are born,
A city that's forever adorned.

From the parks to the museums,
From the markets to the stadiums,
There's always something happening,
An adventure that's always enchanting.

The urban lifestyle, energetic and bold,
A story that's forever told,
Of the people, the places, the sights,
It's a city that's always alight.
