What is a business process?
A business process is a set of defined and structured activities, tasks, or steps that are performed to achieve a specific organizational goal. These processes involve the use of resources, such as people, technology, and information, to optimize, manage and improve business operations, while reducing the overall cost and time. Business processes can range from simple operational tasks such as managing sales, to complex workflows such as managing a supply chain, and are designed to improve efficiency, effectiveness, and productivity within an organization.
