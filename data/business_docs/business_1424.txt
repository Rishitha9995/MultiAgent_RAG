Describe a technology that has had a significant impact in the last five years.
One technology that has had a significant impact in the last five years is Artificial Intelligence (AI). AI technology has undergone rapid advancements, and its application has permeated nearly every sector and industry, transforming the way businesses and people interact with the world around them.

At the core of AI technology is machine learning, which uses algorithms to learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions without explicit programming. This capability has lent itself to numerous possibilities and use cases, such as chatbots, virtual assistants, speech recognition, facial recognition, personalized advertisements, and predictive analytics, among many others.

AI technology has made significant progress in improving data processing and analysis, automating routine tasks, enhancing customer experience, speeding up decision-making, and reducing costs. Additionally, AI has played an essential role in big data and the Internet of Things (IoT), facilitating real-time data analysis and decision-making. 

In summary, AI technology has had a profound impact in the last five years, revolutionizing the way we live, work, communicate, and interact with the world around us, and its influence is only set to grow in the years to come.
