Generate a list of marketing strategies to promote a new mobile app.
Here are some marketing strategies to promote a new mobile app:

1. Social media advertising campaigns: Utilize major social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram and Twitter to reach a large audience and promote the app.

2. App Store Optimization: Optimize the app's title, description, and keywords to rank higher in app store search results.

3. Influencer marketing: Partner with social media influencers and bloggers to spread the word and increase visibility of the app.

4. Content marketing: Develop useful and informative blog posts, videos, and infographics to attract and engage potential customers.

5. Email marketing: Create targeted email campaigns to promote the app to a specific audience.

6. Referral marketing: Encourage current users to refer their friends and family members to the app in exchange for rewards or incentives.

7. In-app advertising: Advertise the new app within other popular apps to reach a large and relevant audience.

8. Press releases: Develop and distribute press releases to relevant media outlets to gain coverage and attention for the app.

9. Search Engine Marketing: Utilize paid search advertising to target specific keywords and drive traffic to the app's landing page.

10. Event marketing: Host or participate in events, conferences, or webinars to engage with potential customers and promote the app.
