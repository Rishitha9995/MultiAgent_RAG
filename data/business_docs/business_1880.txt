Given a table of employee salaries with columns EmployeeID, Salary, and DepartmentID, write a DELETE statement to remove all employees in the Finance department who have a salary less than $50,000.
The DELETE statement would be:

```
DELETE FROM EmployeeSalaries 
WHERE DepartmentID = 'Finance' AND Salary < 50000;
```

Note: Replace the table name `EmployeeSalaries` with the actual name of your table.
