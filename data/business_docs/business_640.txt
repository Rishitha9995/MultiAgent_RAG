Generate a metric to evaluate the customer satisfaction
One way to evaluate customer satisfaction is by using the Net Promoter Score (NPS) metric. NPS is calculated by asking customers to rate, on a scale of 0 to 10, how likely they are to recommend the company or product to others. Respondents are then categorized into three groups: Promoters (9-10), Passives (7-8), and Detractors (0-6). The NPS is then calculated by subtracting the percentage of Detractors from the percentage of Promoters.

NPS = (% of Promoters) - (% of Detractors)

This simple metric provides a reliable measure of customer satisfaction and loyalty, and can help companies identify areas for improvement and track changes in satisfaction over time. Additionally, benchmarking against industry averages or competitors provides further insight into a company's performance in terms of customer satisfaction.
