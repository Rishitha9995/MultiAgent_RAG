Come up with a business strategy to attract new customers.
An effective business strategy for an online clothing store to attract new customers would include the following elements:

1. Social media marketing: Utilize platforms such as Facebook, Instagram and Pinterest to showcase the clothing store's products, run contests and special promotions to engage with potential customers.

2. Influencer partnerships: Partner with fashion bloggers and social media influencers to showcase the brand's clothing, and offer their followers unique discount codes to draw them to the store.

3. Eye-catching website design: Ensure the website is visually appealing, easy to navigate and optimized for mobile users. High-quality photographs of the products can help customers see what they are purchasing.

4. Optimize for search engines: Make sure the website is optimized for search engines, so that it will come up at the top of the results page when potential customers search for relevant keywords.

5. Customer reviews: Encourage customers to leave reviews of their purchases, and showcase them prominently on the website. Positive reviews from other customers can help draw in new customers.

6. Referral program: Implement a referral program where existing customers can earn discounts and rewards for referring their friends and family to the store.

7. Customer service: Make sure the store has excellent customer service, with fast response times, easy returns and exchanges, and a satisfaction guarantee. Happy customers are more likely to spread the word about the store.

By implementing these strategies, the online clothing store can attract new customers and increase its overall customer base.
