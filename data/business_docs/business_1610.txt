Name three ways artificial intelligence is used in healthcare.
1. **Disease diagnosis and treatment**: AI algorithms can analyze vast amounts of patient data and medical records to help doctors identify specific diseases and conditions, make accurate diagnoses, and recommend individualized treatment plans. AI is also being used to develop predictive models to forecast patient outcomes and help doctors make more informed decisions.

2. **Medical imaging analysis**: AI technologies such as deep learning and computer vision are being used to analyze medical images such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans to detect abnormalities and assist radiologists in making more accurate diagnoses. AI systems can also help reduce the workload of radiologists by automating the analysis of routine scans.

3. **Drug discovery and development**: AI is being used to accelerate the process of drug discovery and development by analyzing vast amounts of data on drug-target interactions, predicting how drugs will behave in the human body, and identifying new potential drug candidates. This can help reduce the time, cost, and risk associated with bringing new drugs to market.
