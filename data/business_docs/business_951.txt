Suggest a business idea that is suitable for a person who lives in a rural area and has limited resources.
One business idea that is suitable for a person living in a rural area with limited resources is to start a small-scale agriculture or farming business. This can involve raising livestock, growing fruits and vegetables or other cash crops that are suitable to the local climate and soil. The person can sell their produce and products to the local community, and can also explore the opportunities of selling to nearby urban areas or at farmers' markets. This business can gradually expand by adding value-added products such as jams, pickles, honey, and cheese. This type of business not only makes use of the available resources in a rural area but also promotes self-sufficiency and supports the local economy.
