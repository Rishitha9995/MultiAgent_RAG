What kind of career would suit me best?
Based on your strengths in communication, creativity, and problem-solving, there are several career paths that may be suitable for you. Some possibilities to consider might include:
- Marketing or advertising: where you can utilize your communication skills to convey a message to the general public, and your creativity to design campaigns that capture people's attention.
- Public relations: where you can leverage your communication abilities to maintain a positive image of a company or individual.
- Design or the arts: where you can employ your creativity to produce unique and compelling works.
- Consulting or entrepreneurship: where your problem-solving skills can be used to address challenges and identify opportunities for businesses or organizations.
- Writing or content creation: where your communication and creativity can help you to captivate and engage an audience.

Ultimately, the best career for you will depend on your unique interests, passions, and values. Consider exploring some of these options further to see if they align with your goals and aspirations.
