Name two different ways to reduce poverty in a community.
1. One way to reduce poverty in a community is to provide better access to education and job training programs. This can help equip individuals with the skills and knowledge they need to secure higher paying jobs and increase their earning potential.

2. Another way to reduce poverty in a community is to implement economic development initiatives that focus on creating new job opportunities and supporting local businesses. This can help stimulate the economy, increase employment rates, and provide a sustainable source of income for residents.
