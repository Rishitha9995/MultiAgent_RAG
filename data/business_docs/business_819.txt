Write a to-do list for a job interview.
1. Research the company: Research the company's history, mission, and culture to have a better understanding of the organization.

2. Review the job description: Read through the job responsibilities and requirements to be fully prepared for the interview.

3. Prepare responses to common interview questions: Think about how to answer questions like “Tell me about yourself?” or “What are your strengths and weaknesses?”

4. Choose an appropriate outfit: Dress professionally and appropriately for the company culture.

5. Gather necessary materials: Bring copies of your resume, references, and any other documents such as a portfolio or work samples.

6. Plan your travel route: Ensure you know the exact location of the interview and how long it will take to get there, so you can arrive on time.

7. Practice good body language: Practice good posture, eye contact, and handshakes to make a great first impression.

8. Follow up: After the interview, send a thank-you note or email to the interviewer expressing your appreciation for the opportunity.
