Make a list of 4 natural resources in the environment.
1. Water: An essential natural resource that sustains life on earth and is key in many industrial processes. 
2. Forests: A vital natural resource that provides habitats for wildlife, aids in the regulation of the Earth's climate, supports soil conservation, and acts as a source of timber, fruits, and medicines. 
3. Fossil Fuels: Non-renewable resources, such as coal, oil, and natural gas, which are used as a source of energy to power homes, businesses, and industries. 
4. Minerals: Valuable resources including metals and non-metals that are extracted from the earth, used for construction, manufacturing, and other applications.
