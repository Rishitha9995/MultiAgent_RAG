One popular and highly recommended app for budgeting is "Mint: Budget, Bills, & Finance Tracker". This app allows you to easily track your expenses, create a budget, and set financial goals all in one place. Another option is "You Need a Budget" or YNAB, which is also an effective budgeting tool based on the principle of giving every dollar a job. Both apps are available for iOS and Android devices.
