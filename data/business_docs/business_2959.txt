Explain why investors love Nasdaq.
Investors love Nasdaq for several reasons. 

First, Nasdaq is the world's first electronic stock market, and it provides faster and more efficient trading for investors. Also, it offers a diverse range of products, ranging from common stocks and real-time quotes, to trading services and mutual funds. This makes it easier for investors to access a wide variety of investment options in one place.

Second, Nasdaq is known for its high concentration of technology and internet companies. Since technology is one of the fastest-growing sectors in the market, many investors turn to Nasdaq to tap into this growth potential.

Thirdly, Nasdaq provides a great deal of transparency for investors. The exchange's reporting system makes it easier for investors to obtain important information about companies listed on the exchange. This information can help investors make more informed decisions.

Overall, investors love Nasdaq for its efficiency, diversity, access to technology companies, and transparency. These features make it a very attractive marketplace for investors looking to grow their portfolios.
