Write the biography of a famous person.
Kobe Bryant, born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, was an American professional basketball player who is widely regarded as one of the greatest NBA players of all time. 

Bryant was the son of former NBA player Joe "Jellybean" Bryant and began playing basketball at the age of three. After leading Lower Merion High School to the state championship in 1996, he declared for the NBA draft and was selected 13th overall by the Charlotte Hornets, who subsequently traded him to the Los Angeles Lakers.

Over his 20-year career with the Lakers, Bryant won five NBA championships, two Olympic gold medals, and numerous individual accolades, including an NBA MVP award in 2008, two NBA Finals MVP awards, and 18 All-Star Game selections. Known for his tireless work ethic and competitive nature, Bryant was widely respected by his peers and named to the All-NBA team 15 times.

Off the court, Bryant was known for his philanthropy and was an ambassador for several charities, including the Make-A-Wish Foundation and his own Kobe and Vanessa Bryant Family Foundation. He also wrote a book, won an Academy Award for his animated short film "Dear Basketball," and founded Kobe Inc., a media company focused on inspiring young athletes.

Tragically, Bryant's life was cut short on January 26, 2020 when he died at the age of 41 in a helicopter crash that also claimed the lives of his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna, and seven others. His loss was deeply felt around the world and he remains widely celebrated for his accomplishments and contributions to the game of basketball.
