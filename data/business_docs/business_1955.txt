List three tips for better time management.
1. **Prioritize tasks**: Determine which tasks are the most important and need to be done first. Focus on these high-priority tasks before moving on to lower-priority ones. This can help you to make sure that the most critical tasks are completed in time.

2. **Plan ahead**: Take some time at the beginning of each day, week or month to plan your tasks and activities. Use a calendar, planner or app to schedule your tasks and appointments so that you have a clear overview of what needs to be done and when. This can help you to avoid any surprises or last-minute stress.

3. **Avoid distractions**: Distractions such as social media, emails or phone calls can significantly decrease your productivity. Set specific times during the day to check your emails or phone messages, and try to avoid any non-essential activities during your work time. This can help you to stay focused and get more done in less time.
