Identify the fields in which IoT is used.
IoT (Internet of Things) is used in various fields which include but are not limited to the following:

1. Home automation - IoT devices are widely used to automate and control various systems and appliances in homes, such as lighting, heating, and air conditioning.

2. Healthcare - IoT technology is transforming healthcare with remote health monitoring, telemedicine, and other applications.

3. Agriculture - IoT devices are used in precision farming to improve crop yield, reduce waste, and increase efficiency.

4. Manufacturing - IoT is used in the manufacturing industry to optimize production processes, improve supply chain management, and reduce costs.

5. Transportation - IoT is being used in transportation to improve traffic flow, reduce fuel consumption, and increase safety.

6. Energy - IoT technology is used in the energy sector to optimize energy consumption, reduce waste, and improve efficiency.

7. Retail - IoT is transforming the retail industry with smart shopping carts, personalized marketing, and inventory management applications.

8. Environmental monitoring - IoT devices are used to monitor the environment, to help reduce pollution, and to improve resource management.
