Entrepreneurship is the process of identifying opportunities, taking calculated risks and creating a new business. It involves envisioning a new idea and bringing it to life, thus creating jobs and adding value to the economy. Successful entrepreneurs are innovative and persistent, and they possess strong leadership qualities, which they use to achieve their goals. Entrepreneurship is not without challenges, but with passion, hard work, and determination, it can lead to personal and financial fulfillment.
