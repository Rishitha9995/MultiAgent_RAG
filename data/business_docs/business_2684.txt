Design an outline for a five-minute presentation on the topic of “How to Reduce Carbon Emissions”
I. Introduction (30 seconds)
    a. Explanation of carbon emissions
    b. The present and future consequences of carbon emissions
    c. Importance of reducing carbon emissions for a better future

II. The Role of Governments in reducing carbon emissions (1 minute)
    a. Setting up policies and regulations
    b. Implementing carbon taxes
    c. Encouraging research and innovation

III. The Role of Businesses (1 minute)
    a. Adopting and promoting green technologies and practices
    b. Investment in renewable energy sources
    c. Increasing energy efficiency

IV. The Role of Individuals (1 minute)
    a. Reducing energy consumption
    b. Choosing public transportation, carpooling, biking or other sustainable modes of transport
    c. Eating local, plant-based, and organic foods
    
V. Collaborative Efforts Towards a Greener Future (1 minute)
    a. Working together towards a common goal
    b. Encouraging others to adopt sustainable practices
    c. Spreading awareness about the significance of reducing carbon emissions

VI. Conclusion (30 seconds)
    a. Recap of the key points made in the presentation
    b. The need for immediate action
    c. Call to action for a better and sustainable future.
