Name three things a person needs for a successful job hunt.
1. A well-written resume or CV: A well-crafted resume or CV provides a clear and concise overview of a person's qualifications, skillsets, and experiences. It makes the first impression on potential employers and is a crucial element of a successful job hunt.

2. Preparation for job interviews: Preparation for job interviews is essential as it shows the employer that the candidate is serious about the job and is enthusiastic about joining the company. Common preparation techniques include researching the company and position, preparing answers for common interview questions, and dressing professionally.

3. Networking: Networking can be an effective way to find job opportunities that may not be publicized. Building and maintaining professional relationships and utilizing social media platforms like LinkedIn can help job seekers learn about potential job openings, connect with potential employers or colleagues, and expand their job search possibilities.
