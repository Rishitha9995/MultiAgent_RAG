Brainstorm 3 possible applications of machine learning.
1. Predictive Maintenance: Machine learning can be used to predict when a machine is likely to fail or require maintenance. This would allow for timely repair and minimize downtime, thus leading to cost savings and improved operational efficiency for businesses.

2. Healthcare Diagnosis: Advanced machine learning techniques can be used to analyze medical images, patient data and other diagnostic information to aid in the diagnosis of diseases. Such tools can help doctors to make more accurate and timely medical decisions, thus improving patient outcomes.

3. Personalized Advertising: By analyzing large amounts of data related to an individual's browsing and purchasing behavior, machine learning algorithms can identify patterns and make predictions about their preferences. This information can then be leveraged to serve personalized and more relevant ads to consumers, thus leading to improved engagement and conversion rates for businesses.
