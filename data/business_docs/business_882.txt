Identify 5 common symptoms of the flu.
Five common symptoms of the flu include:

1. Fever: A high temperature is one of the most common symptoms of the flu. Individuals with flu may experience a fever of 100 F (37.8 C) or higher.
2. Body aches: Flu often causes achy muscles and joints throughout the body, especially in the back, arms, and legs.
3. Headaches: Many people with the flu experience headaches, which may also be accompanied by light sensitivity, and congestion.
4. Cough and sore throat: A persistent cough and sore throat are also common symptoms of the flu. Chest discomfort and pain may also accompany the cough.
5. Fatigue and weakness: Individuals with the flu often experience extreme fatigue and weakness, making it difficult to perform regular activities. This feeling of tiredness and exhaustion can last for a few weeks even after other symptoms subside.
