Write a short paragraph about time management.
Time management is the process of organizing and planning how to effectively use the time available to accomplish specific tasks, goals or projects. Good time management helps individuals to work smarter, not harder, thereby achieving more in less time, even when time is tight and the pressure is high. Effective time management involves setting SMART goals, prioritizing tasks, creating a schedule, avoiding distractions, and taking breaks. By implementing proper time management techniques, one can increase productivity, reduce stress and improve their overall quality of life.
