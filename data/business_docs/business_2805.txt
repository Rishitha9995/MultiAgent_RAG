Describe how cryptocurrencies work in a few sentences.
Cryptocurrencies are decentralized digital currencies that use encryption techniques to secure their transactions and to control the creation of new units. They operate on blockchain technology, which is a distributed ledger that records all transactions across a network of computers. Transactions are verified by network nodes and recorded in blocks, which are linked and secured using cryptography. The supply of most cryptocurrencies is controlled by an algorithm, and their value is determined by supply and demand in the market.
