Describe two steps that can help to reduce carbon dioxide emissions
There are several steps that can help to reduce carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions, but here are two effective actions:

1. Switch to clean energy sources: One of the greatest contributors to CO2 emissions is the burning of fossil fuels for energy generation. By switching to cleaner, renewable energy sources such as solar, wind or hydro power, the amount of CO2 released into the atmosphere can be significantly reduced. Governments, businesses and individuals can all take steps to move away from fossil fuel dependency and invest in low or zero emission power generation.

2. Improve energy efficiency: Reducing the amount of energy required to perform various tasks can significantly reduce CO2 emissions. This can be accomplished through a variety of means, such as upgrading to energy efficient appliances, utilizing smart building technology to reduce energy consumption, or simply turning off lights and electronics when not in use. By using energy more efficiently, we decrease our demand on energy sources that produce CO2 emissions, and help to lessen our overall impact on the environment.
