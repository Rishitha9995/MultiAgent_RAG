Explain what is meant by the term “segmentation” in marketing.
Segmentation in marketing refers to the process of dividing a larger market into smaller groups or segments of customers with similar interests, behaviors, or characteristics. The goal of segmentation is to identify groups of potential customers who are more likely to respond positively to specific marketing efforts and to tailor marketing messages and strategies to more effectively reach and engage these groups. Segmentation can be based on a wide variety of factors, including geographic, demographic, psychographic, and behavioral data. By understanding the needs, wants, and behaviors of these different segments, businesses can more effectively target their marketing and sales efforts, resulting in more efficient use of resources and greater success in reaching potential customers.
