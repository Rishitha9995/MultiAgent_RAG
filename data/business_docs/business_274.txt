Please provide two disadvantages of using machine learning algorithms
1. **Limited Interpretability** - Machine learning algorithms often act as a black box and it might be difficult to understand how the model has made a prediction or decision. This means that valuable insights into data relationships or correlations might be lost, as the algorithm simply makes predictions or decisions without providing any explanation. This lack of transparency and interpretation can make it hard for businesses or researchers to determine the effectiveness of the model or the validity of the results.

2. **Data quality and quantity issues** - Machine learning algorithms relies heavily on the quality and quantity of the data provided to train the model. Poor data quality, such as inaccurate, inconsistent or irrelevant data, can result in inaccurate predictions and decisions. This is why it’s incredibly important to have good quality and properly validated data prior to training your model. Also, if there is not enough data available, the model may be overfitted to the existing data and may not generalize well to new data or situations.
