Tell the story of the impact of coronavirus on the US economy in five sentences.
In March 2020, the novel coronavirus forced the shutdown of businesses and other economic activities across the United States, leading to a historic and widespread economic impact. Millions of Americans lost their jobs or had their work hours reduced as a result of the pandemic, causing record-high levels of unemployment. The federal government responded with an unprecedented level of economic stimulus, passing trillions of dollars in relief packages to assist individuals, small businesses, and major industries. Despite these efforts, the economy contracted sharply and the recovery has been slow and uneven, with certain sectors, such as hospitality and travel, continuing to struggle. The full economic impact of COVID-19 is still unfolding, and it remains to be seen how long it will take for the US economy to fully recover.
