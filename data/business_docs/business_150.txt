Name three things that a bookkeeper needs to do.
1. Record Financial Transactions: A bookkeeper is responsible for recording and maintaining complete and accurate financial transactions, including purchases, sales, receipts, and payments, in a systematic manner.

2. Reconcile Accounts: A bookkeeper needs to reconcile accounts, matching the transactions recorded with bank statements or other financial statements, to ensure the accuracy and completeness of the records.

3. Prepare Financial Reports: A bookkeeper is also responsible for preparing financial reports, such as income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements, which provide important information about the financial health of a business.
