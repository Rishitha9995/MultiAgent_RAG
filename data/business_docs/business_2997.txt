What is digital marketing?
Digital marketing refers to the promotion of products, services, or brands through digital channels, such as search engines, social media, websites, email, and mobile apps. It encompasses a wide range of tactics and strategies aimed at reaching and engaging with customers through digital technologies. Some of the key elements of digital marketing include search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click advertising, content marketing, social media marketing, and email marketing. Digital marketing allows businesses to reach a larger audience in a more targeted and cost-effective way compared to traditional marketing methods.
