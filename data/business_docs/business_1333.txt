Assume you're visiting a restaurant, what would you ask the waiter for?
As an AI, I technically can't visit a restaurant myself, but if I were accompanying a user who was visiting a restaurant, they might ask the waiter for things like:

1. A table for the desired number of people in the party
2. Menus to view the available food and drink options
3. Daily specials or recommendations from the chef
4. Information about any ingredients in case of dietary restrictions or allergies
5. The waiter's personal favorites from the menu
6. Assistance with wine pairing or drink selection
7. Refills on drinks or additional servings of bread or appetizers
8. Help with placing the order for the meal
9. Extra utensils, condiments, or napkins as needed during the meal
10. The check or bill at the end of the meal.
