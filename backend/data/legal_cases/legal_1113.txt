During the term of her employment under this Agreement, the Executive shall be permitted to participate in any group life, hospitalization or disability insurance plans, health (including dental) programs, pension and profit sharing plans and similar benefits that may be available to other senior executives of the Company generally, on the same terms as may be applicable to such other executives, in each case to the extent that the Executive is eligible under the terms of such plans or programs. During the term of her employment under this Agreement, the Company or the Trust, as applicable, shall maintain customary liability insurance for trustees and officers and list the Executive as a covered officer.
