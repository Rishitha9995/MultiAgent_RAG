Neither party shall have the right to assign its rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party; provided, however, that either party may, without such consent, assign this Agreement and its rights and obligations hereunder (a) to any Affiliate, or (b) in connection with the transfer or sale of all or substantially all of its business to which this Agreement relates, or in the event of its merger, consolidation, change of control or similar transaction. Any permitted assignee shall assume all obligations of its assignor under this Agreement. Any purported assignment, transfer or delegation in violation of this Section 6.6 shall be void.
