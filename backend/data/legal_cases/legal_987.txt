On the Restatement Effective Date, the Borrower shall pay (i) to the Administrative Agent, for the accounts of the Lenders, the fees payable pursuant to Section 2.5 and the first sentence of Section 3.3 of the Existing Credit Agreement which have accrued for the period from the last date such fees were paid to but excluding the Restatement Effective Date and (ii) for the account of the Administrative Agent and the Lenders entitled thereto, the fees referred to in Section 5.1(h) of the Restated Credit Agreement. The fees described in this Section 7 shall be payable in immediately available funds. Once paid, such fees shall not be refundable under any circumstances.
