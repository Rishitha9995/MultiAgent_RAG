Change in Control .  Any person or group of persons (within the meaning of Section 13(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended) shall, directly or indirectly, obtain beneficial ownership or control in one or more series of transactions of more than thirty percent (30%) of the common stock or thirty percent (30%) of the voting power of the Borrower entitled to vote in the election of members of the board of directors of the Borrower or there shall have occurred under any indenture or other instrument evidencing the Permitted Senior Notes (if any) or   any other Debt in excess of $10,000,000 any “change in control” or equivalent term (as defined in such indenture or other evidence of Debt) obligating the Borrower to repurchase, redeem or repay all or any part of the Debt or Capital Stock provided for therein (any such event, a “ Change in Control ”).
