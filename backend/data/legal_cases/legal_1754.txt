The Borrower and each of its Subsidiaries will maintain with financially sound and reputable insurance companies insurance and/or make provisions for self‑insurance in such amounts and against such risks as are usually carried by companies engaged in similar business and as are consistent with the prudent operation of its business. The Borrower will furnish to the Administrative Agent, upon written request of the Administrative Agent or any Lender, reasonable information as to the insurance carried; provided , however , such requests shall be limited to twice per calendar year in the aggregate.
