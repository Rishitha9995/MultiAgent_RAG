All notices to be given hereunder shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been duly given on: the date personally or hand delivered; one (1) day after being sent by internationally-recognized overnight delivery courier; and three (3) days after being sent by certified mail, return receipt requested. Notices mailed to Executive shall be sent to Executive’s last home address as reflected in the Company’s personnel records. Executive promptly shall notify Company of any change in Executive’s address. Notices to be issued to the Company shall be directed to the CEO and shall be mailed to the Company’s headquarters.
