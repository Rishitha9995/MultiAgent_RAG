Each Borrower will not, and will not permit any Subsidiary to, create or acquire any additional Subsidiary unless such Borrower gives prior written notice to the Administrative Agent of such creation or acquisition and complies with Section  10.1.13 . Each Borrower shall not, and shall not permit any Restricted Subsidiary to, sell, assign or otherwise dispose of any Equity Interests in any Subsidiary except in compliance with Section 10.2.9(c) and except that Equity Interests in Foreign Subsidiaries owned on the Closing Date may be sold, assigned or otherwise disposed of in connection with Asset Dispositions permitted by Section  10.2.9(k) .
