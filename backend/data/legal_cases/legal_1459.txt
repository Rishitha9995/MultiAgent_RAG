At the closing of the Transfer of a Membership Interest pursuant to Sections 3.6(b) or 3.6(c) , the Transferee shall deliver to the Transferring Member the full consideration required by this Agreement. Any transfer or similar taxes involved in such sale shall be paid by the Transferring Member, and the Transferring Member shall provide the Transferee with such evidence of the Transferring Member’s authority to Transfer hereunder and such tax lien waivers and similar instruments as the Transferee may reasonably request.
