On the Termination Date Lender shall, at Borrower’s expense, release or terminate any filings or other agreements that perfect Lender’s security interest, upon Lender’s receipt of the following, in form and content satisfactory to Lender: (i) a release of all claims against Lender by Borrower relating to Lender’s performance and obligations under the Loan Documents, and (ii) an agreement by Borrower, and any new lender to Borrower to indemnify Lender for any payments received by Lender that are applied to the Obligations as a final payoff that may subsequently be returned or otherwise not paid for any reason.
