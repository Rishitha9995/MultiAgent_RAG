All the accounts, books, registers, ledgers, the Company Board minutes and financial and other records of whatsoever kind of the Company have been fully, properly and accurately kept and completed; there are no material inaccuracies or discrepancies of any kind contained or reflected therein; and they give and reflect a true and fair view of the financial, contractual and legal position of the Company.
