The Borrower shall use the proceeds of the Loans only (i) to Refinance the outstanding Debt under the Existing Credit Agreement and (ii) to pay the fees, expenses and costs of the Transactions. No part of the proceeds of any Loan will be used, whether directly or indirectly, (x) for any purpose that would violate any of the regulations of the Board, including Regulations T, U and X or (y) to finance the purchase or carry of margin stock (within the meaning of Regulation T, U or X of the Board).
