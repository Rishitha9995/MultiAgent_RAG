Subject to Sections 2(b) and 2(c), one share of common stock of the Company (the “Common Stock”) shall be issuable for each Unit subject to the Award (the “Shares”) upon vesting. Immediately thereafter, or as soon as administratively feasible, the Company will deliver the appropriate number of Shares to the Director after satisfaction of any required tax or other withholding obligations, or, in the case of Approved 102 Option, to the Trustee. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the relevant number of Shares shall be delivered to the Director or, in the case of Approved 102 Option, to the Trustee no later than March 15th of the year following the calendar year in which the Award vests.
