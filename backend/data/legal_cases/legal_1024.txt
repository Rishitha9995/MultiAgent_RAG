Subject to Sections 3 and 4, in order to vest in the RSUs, the Grantee must continue as an active employee of an Employing Company for three years from the Date of Grant, subject to the Employing Company’s right to terminate the Grantee’s employment at any time for any reason. The RSUs shall vest on the three-year anniversary of the Date of Grant, provided that the Grantee is continuously employed by an Employing Company through such anniversary.
