Subject to the provisions of Sections 3 and 4 of this Agreement, the Company will pay or cause to be paid to the Executive so long as the Executive is employed hereunder a base salary equal to $800,000 per annum (the “ Base Salary ”). Base Salary may be adjusted to reflect any changes made to the Executive’s Base Salary in the payroll records of the Company and agreed to by the Company and the Executive, and the Executive’s Base Salary, as so adjusted. The timing of payments pursuant to this Section 2(a) will be made on a basis consistent with CBRE’s general payroll practices.
