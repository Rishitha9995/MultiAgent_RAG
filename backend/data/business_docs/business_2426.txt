Make a list of common elements present in a modern web page.
Here is a list of common elements present in a modern web page:

1. Header: A header usually contains the logo, company name, and navigation menu.
2. Navigation Menu: A navigation menu is a list of links that directs users to the different pages or sections of a website.
3. Banner or Hero Image: A banner or hero image is a large image or slideshow, usually placed at the top of a web page, that showcases the website's main message or product.
4. Main Content: The main content area contains the bulk of the information on a web page, such as text, images, and videos.
5. Sidebar: A sidebar is an optional element that usually contains additional information or options, such as a search bar, links to other pages or recent posts, ads, or social media feeds.
6. Footer: The footer is located at the bottom of the page and usually contains copyright information, contact details, and links to the privacy policy and terms of use.
7. Call-to-Action: A call-to-action is a button or link that encourages users to take a specific action, such as "Sign Up" or "Learn More."
8. Forms: Forms are used to collect information from users, such as contact details or feedback.
9. Social Media Icons: Social media icons are links to a company's social media profiles, such as Facebook or Twitter.
10. Media: Media elements such as images, videos, and audio can be embedded into a web page to enhance the user experience.
