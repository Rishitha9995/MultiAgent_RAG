I want to get a new phone. Pick 3 phones to compare and explain why they would be good choices.
Based on my research, three great phones to compare are the Samsung Galaxy S21 5G, the Apple iPhone 12 Pro, and the Google Pixel 5.

1) Samsung Galaxy S21 5G: The new Samsung flagship phone is packed with features that make it a great choice for anyone in the market for a new phone. It has a colorful 6.2-inch OLED display, runs on a powerful Snapdragon 888 processor, and sports 5G. This phone's camera setup is one of the best in the market, including a 64-megapixel telephoto lens and 8K video recording. If you're an Android fan looking for a speedy and sleek phone packed with features, the Samsung Galaxy S21 5G is a great option.

2) Apple iPhone 12 Pro: The Apple iPhone 12 Pro is the latest addition to Apple's premium line of smartphones. It features a gorgeous 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, a powerful A14 Bionic chip, and 5G connectivity. The phone’s camera setup includes a 12-megapixel ultra-wide lens, a 12-megapixel wide-angle lens, and a 12-megapixel telephoto lens, making it great for capturing photos and videos. If you're an Apple fan looking for a new phone that is powerful, stylish, and packed with features, the iPhone 12 Pro is an excellent choice.

3) Google Pixel 5: The Google Pixel 5 is a great phone for anyone who values simplicity and pure Android experience. It has a 6-inch OLED display, runs on a Snapdragon 765G processor, and comes with 5G connectivity. The Pixel's standout feature is its camera, which captures excellent pictures even in challenging lighting conditions. If you're looking for a phone with a clean and simple interface, top-notch camera, and access to the latest Android updates, the Google Pixel 5 is worth considering.
