Explain the meaning of 'procrastination'.
Procrastination refers to the action of delaying or postponing tasks, responsibilities or decisions that need to be completed or made. It is often seen as a negative habit that can lead to decreased productivity, stress and anxiety. People may procrastinate for various reasons such as fear of failure, lack of motivation, feeling overwhelmed, or poor time management skills. Overcoming procrastination may involve identifying its causes and developing strategies to stay focused, motivated, and productive.
