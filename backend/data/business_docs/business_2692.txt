Suggest a creative way to get more customers to follow the company on social media platforms.
One creative way to increase the number of customers following the company on social media platforms could be to launch a digital scavenger hunt that takes place across the company's various social media platforms. Participants in the scavenger hunt would be required to follow the company on each platform and complete tasks, such as sharing posts or tagging friends, in order to find clues and progress through the game. To incentivize participation, the company could offer prizes, such as discounts or exclusive opportunities, to the first few customers who successfully complete the scavenger hunt. This interactive and engaging campaign would not only increase the company's followers but also boost customer engagement and brand loyalty.
