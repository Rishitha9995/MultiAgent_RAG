Name five popular computer software
1. Microsoft Windows – an operating system used in many personal computers and laptops worldwide.
2. Microsoft Office – a suite of productivity applications like Word, Excel, PowerPoint used in businesses and homes.
3. Google Chrome – a widely used web browser, known for its fast and reliable performance.
4. Adobe Photoshop – an advanced image editing software used for photo manipulation, graphic design and digital art.
5. Anti-virus Software like McAfee or Norton Security – programs that protect computers from malware, viruses, and phishing attacks.
