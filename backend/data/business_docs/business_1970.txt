Enumerate why businesses should develop an effective digital marketing strategy.
1. Expanding Reach: A digital marketing strategy allows a business to expand its reach to a larger audience through various online platforms including social media, search engines, and e-mail marketing.

2. Measurable Results: With digital marketing, businesses can track the progress and efficacy of their campaigns and strategies through analytics, providing measurable results and insights into customer behavior.

3. Cost Effective: Digital marketing is generally more cost-effective than traditional forms of marketing, allowing businesses to achieve higher returns on investment.

4. Targeted Marketing: By using advanced targeting techniques, businesses can reach their ideal audience through demographics, interests, behavior, and more, allowing for more efficient use of resources.

5. Brand Building: A strong online presence through digital marketing can help businesses establish and build their brand, increasing awareness, credibility and customer loyalty.

6. Improved Engagement: Through interactive digital channels, businesses can engage with their customers in real-time, allowing for improved customer experience and feedback.

7. Increased Conversions: A well-implemented digital marketing strategy can drive traffic and increase conversions, helping businesses generate more leads and sales.

8. Global Reach: With the internet, businesses can reach a global audience, allowing them to expand beyond their local geography and tap into new markets.

9. Adaptability: Digital marketing provides businesses with the flexibility to quickly adapt to changes in the market or consumer behavior, allowing them to respond and adjust their strategies accordingly.

10. Competitive Advantage: A well-developed digital marketing strategy can provide a competitive advantage, allowing businesses to stay ahead of their competitors and stand out in the market.
