Analyze the rhetorical device and its effect in the following sentence.
The rhetorical device used in this sentence is a metaphor. Specifically, it is an idiomatic expression meaning that he made a large profit or gained a significant financial success from investing in the stock market. The effect of this rhetorical device is to emphasize the magnitude of the person's success by drawing a comparison between gaining financial success and killing something--which, in another context, can indicate a total domination or achieving the utmost result. This comparison creates a stronger impact and adds more color to the language.
