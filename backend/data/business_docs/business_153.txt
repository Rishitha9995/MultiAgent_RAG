Point out 3 implications that the given phenomenon has had on society.
1. *Increased Flexibility and Autonomy*: Remote working has provided employees with more autonomy over their work schedule, allowing them to balance professional and personal responsibilities better. People are now able to work from anywhere, anytime when their role and work policies allow it.

2. *Altering Traditional Office Dynamics*: With remote working on the rise, traditional office dynamics and requirements have shifted. Companies are rethinking the way they manage office spaces, team collaborations, and employee communication. This change has significantly reduced overheads, saved commuting time, and increased productivity.

3. *Changing Labor Market*: The rise of remote working has resulted in an increasingly competitive labor market. Employers now have access to a more extensive talent pool not restricted by geographic limitations, while employees have more opportunities to work for companies based in different cities or countries. This approach has also promoted greater workforce diversity, encouraging the inclusion of underrepresented groups, creating more opportunities, and promoting equality.
