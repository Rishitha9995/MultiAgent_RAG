List five physical health benefits associated with playing sports.
1. Improved cardiovascular health: Playing sports helps improve heart function and reduces the risk of heart disease, high blood pressure, and stroke.

2. Increased strength and endurance: Participation in sports helps build stronger muscles and increase endurance through regular physical activity and training.

3. Better weight management: Sports can burn calories and help regulate weight by increasing metabolism and promoting lean muscle mass.

4. Enhanced flexibility and coordination: Sports such as gymnastics, martial arts, and dance require flexibility and coordination. These skills carry over to other aspects of life and can improve overall physical function.

5. Stronger bones: Weight-bearing activities such as running, jumping, and lifting weights stimulate bone growth and increase bone density, reducing the risk of osteoporosis in later life.
