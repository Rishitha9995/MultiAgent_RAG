Suggest a way to improve an existing product
One way to improve an existing handheld gaming console could be by increasing its battery life. This can be done by using more efficient power management technology or implementing a larger battery while keeping the overall design sleek and portable. Another improvement could be better ergonomics, providing players with a more comfortable grip for extended gaming sessions. Additionally, incorporating more advanced haptic feedback technology could enhance the gaming experience by providing players with more immersive sensory feedback.
