Name five different websites that offer online streaming of movies and shows.
Five different websites that offer online streaming of movies and shows are:

1. Netflix - An on-demand subscription-based streaming service that offers a wide selection of movies, TV shows, and original series.

2. Amazon Prime Video - An on-demand streaming service included as part of Amazon's Prime membership, offering movies, TV shows, and original content.

3. Hulu - An on-demand subscription-based streaming service that offers movies and TV shows, as well as live TV options.

4. Disney + - A direct-to-consumer streaming service owned and operated by The Walt Disney Company, offering movies, TV shows, and original content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic.

5. HBO Max - A subscription-based streaming service that offers movies, TV shows, and original series from HBO, in addition to content from WarnerMedia's other brands such as DC, Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, and more.
