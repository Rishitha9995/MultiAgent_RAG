Explain why a company would choose the pay-per-click (PPC) advertising model.
A company may choose the pay-per-click (PPC) advertising model for several reasons. 

Firstly, PPC advertising can provide immediate and targeted traffic to a company's website. By bidding on relevant keywords, a company can display its advertisements to users who are actively searching for their products or services. This targeted traffic is often more likely to convert into sales or leads, providing a higher return on investment for the advertising spend.

Secondly, PPC advertising allows a company to have greater control over its advertising budget. With PPC, the company is only charged when a user clicks on their advertisement, so they can set a maximum budget for their campaign and be sure not to exceed it. This way, the campaigns can be easily scaled up or down depending on the company's available resources.

Thirdly, PPC advertising offers measurable and trackable results. Companies can track which keywords, ads, and targeting options deliver the most clicks, conversions, and sales. This information can then be used to optimize future campaigns and improve their overall effectiveness.

In summary, the pay-per-click advertising model offers immediate and targeted traffic, budget control, and measurable results, making it an attractive option for companies looking to advertise their products or services.
