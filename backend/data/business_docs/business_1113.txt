Identify the biggest challenge facing the given industry.
The automotive manufacturing industry is currently facing several challenges, but the most prominent one is the shift towards electric and autonomous vehicles. This shift requires significant investment in research and development to produce vehicles that meet the new standards for emissions, efficiency, and safety. It also requires manufacturers to adapt their production processes and supply chains to accommodate the new technology, as well as retraining their workforce to operate and maintain the new vehicles. This presents a major challenge for traditional automotive manufacturers, as it requires them to drastically change their business models and potentially disrupt their established market positions. Failure to keep up with the rapidly changing industry could result in lost market share and reduced profits.
