List three qualities for a successful entrepreneur.
1. **Perseverance:** Being an entrepreneur often means facing obstacles and setbacks. A successful entrepreneur is someone who has the ability to overcome adversity, continue to move forward and not give up on their vision and goals.

2. **Adaptability:** The business landscape is constantly changing, and entrepreneurs must be able to adapt to those changes. A successful entrepreneur is someone who is flexible, open to new ideas and able to make quick decisions to respond to changing conditions.

3. **Creativity:** Entrepreneurship often involves identifying problems and coming up with innovative solutions. A successful entrepreneur is someone who can think outside the box, approach problems from new angles, and generate fresh and original ideas.
