Generate a list of new features that could be added to a task management app.
1. Time tracking: A feature that allows users to track the amount of time spent on a particular task or project.
2. Collaboration: The ability to share and assign tasks with other users, including commenting and real-time collaboration on tasks.
3. Prioritization: A system for organizing tasks based on their level of importance, urgency or deadline.
4. Reminders and Notifications: Alerts to remind users of upcoming tasks, deadlines and scheduled events.
5. Goal setting: A feature that allows users to set long-term goals, break them down into smaller tasks and track progress towards achieving them.
6. Integrations: Integration with other productivity tools and apps, such as calendars, email, and note-taking apps.
7. Customization: The ability to customize the user interface or layout and create personalized categories and labels.
8. Voice commands: The ability to interact with the app using voice commands or voice-activated assistants like Siri or Alexa.
9. Productivity reports: The ability to generate reports on productivity, time management, and goal progress.
10. Offline access: The ability to access and use the app offline, without an active internet connection.
