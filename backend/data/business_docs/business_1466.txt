Generate one example of a machine learning solution.
One example of a machine learning solution is a recommendation engine for an e-commerce website. The goal of the system is to suggest relevant products to customers, increasing their satisfaction and loyalty, as well as boosting sales for the company. Utilizing data such as previous customer purchases, items that have been added to their cart, and their browsing history, the system can learn to suggest items that are relevant and of potential interest to the customer. The more data the system ingests, the better its predictions become, and over time the recommendations become increasingly personalized to each individual user.
