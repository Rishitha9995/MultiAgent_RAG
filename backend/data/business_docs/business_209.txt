Generate a definition of sentiment analysis.
Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, is the process of identifying and extracting subjective information from text, such as determining the underlying emotional tone or attitude expressed. It is commonly used in social media monitoring, customer feedback analysis, and market research to determine general opinions about a product or issue. Algorithms and natural language processing techniques are used to classify text as positive, negative, or neutral, and more advanced techniques can identify specific emotions or opinions.
