Name 5 techniques for effective time management.
Five techniques for effective time management are:

1. Prioritization: Identify the most important tasks and focus on completing them first. By distinguishing the urgent and essential from the less important, you can plan and allocate time accordingly.

2. Make a schedule: Create a schedule, and stick to it by outlining daily, weekly, and monthly tasks, along with timeframes for completion. This will provide structure to your day and can improve productivity.

3. Eliminate or minimize distractions: Avoid or minimize distractions while working to improve focus and efficiency. This could mean turning off notifications, closing unnecessary tabs or apps, or working in a quiet and conducive environment.

4. Take breaks: Taking short breaks throughout the day can help you stay fresh and focused. A break can provide a much-needed mental rest and can help re-energize you for the remaining task.

5. Plan ahead: Take a little time at the end of each day or week to plan and organize for the upcoming days or week. This will help you avoid last-minute surprises and can help you stay on track with your time management goals.
