Summarize the benefits of walking as a form of exercise.
Walking is a low-impact form of exercise that has numerous benefits for physical and mental health. It improves cardiovascular health and can reduce the risk of developing heart disease and hypertension. It also helps improve muscle strength and endurance, especially in the legs, hips, and core. Additionally, regular walking can help with weight management and improving overall body composition. Walking also has mental health benefits, helping to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression, and can improve cognitive function and mood. It's an easy and accessible form of exercise that can be done almost anywhere, making it a great option for people of all ages and abilities.
