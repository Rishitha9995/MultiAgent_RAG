Explain what sales forecasting is.
Sales forecasting is the process of estimating future sales by analyzing and interpreting historical data and market trends to make informed predictions. It involves using various statistical methods and models to make projections about the demand for a product or service over a defined period of time. This information is important to businesses because it allows them to make informed decisions about inventory management, production planning, budgeting, and other aspects of their operations. By accurately forecasting sales, a company can reduce its risks, optimize its resource allocation, and improve its chances of meeting its financial goals.
