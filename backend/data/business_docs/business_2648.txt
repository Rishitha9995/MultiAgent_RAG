Name five questions someone might ask before starting a business.
1. What are the market demands and challenges for the type of business I am considering? 
2. Who is my target audience and how will I reach them? 
3. How much capital will be required to start and sustain the business until it becomes profitable? 
4. Who are my competitors and what differentiates my business from theirs? 
5. What type of legal and financial requirements do I need to fulfill to establish and operate the business?
