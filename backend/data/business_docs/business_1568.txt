Design a new type of board game.
Game Title: Pathway to Prosperity

**Objective:** Pathway to Prosperity is a board game in which the goal is to become the wealthiest player by investing in various industries, acquiring businesses and making strategic financial decisions.

**Number of Players:** 2-6 players

**Age Range:** 12 and up

**Game Contents:**
- Game board
- 6 player tokens
- Play Money
- Investment cards
- Business cards
- Chance cards
- 2 dice

**Gameplay:** Each player begins the game with a set amount of money and chooses their player token. The game board is designed as a winding path with various spaces designated for different actions such as: investing in a specific industry, purchasing or acquiring a business, paying taxes, and chance spaces. 

Players roll the dice to determine how many spaces they move on their turn. When a player lands on an investment or business space, they have the option to purchase the investment or business if they have the necessary funds. If another player already owns the business, the player must pay the owner rent. 

Chance spaces allow players to draw a chance card which could result in either a positive or negative outcome such as receiving money, losing money, or being sent to a specific space on the board. 

The game also includes events that can impact the industries and businesses such as economic booms or recessions, technological advancements, or natural disasters. 

As players progress and make strategic decisions, they have the chance to increase their wealth and gain financial stability. The game ends when one player reaches the designated monetary amount or when all players agree to end the game. The player with the highest net worth is declared the winner.

**Skills acquired:** strategic thinking, risk-taking, financial management, and decision-making abilities.


Pathway to prosperity provides hours of strategic game play while teaching valuable lessons about finance and business management.
