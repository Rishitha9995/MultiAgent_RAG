Write a paragraph comparing the economy of two countries
Germany and France are two of the largest economies in Europe, with Germany having the largest economy in the European Union and the fourth largest in the world, while France has the seventh largest economy globally. Both countries follow a mixed-market model, where the government and private businesses work together in shaping the economy. Germany has a strong focus on exports and manufacturing, particularly automobiles and machinery, while France's economy is diverse and driven by sectors such as agriculture, tourism, and a wide range of industries. Germany has a higher GDP per capita than France, but their unemployment rates are similar. Overall, both countries have strong and stable economies, but with different strengths and focuses.
