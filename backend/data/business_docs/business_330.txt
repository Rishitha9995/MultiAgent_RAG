Give a short description of the given event.
The World Economic Forum (WEF) is an annual event that brings together leaders from business, politics, academia and other sectors to discuss global economic issues and seek solutions to common challenges. The event, founded in 1971, is held in Davos, Switzerland, and has become a high-profile platform for international collaboration and decision-making. It also facilitates public-private cooperation and encourages the sharing of ideas, insights, and best practices in various fields.
