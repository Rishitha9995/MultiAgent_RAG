This highlights the potential for blockchain technology to become more integrated into mainstream culture, with celebrity endorsements serving as a powerful driving force.

With an increasing number of developers building decentralized applications on the Ethereum network, it is clear that the potential use cases for blockchain technology are far-reaching.

However, there is also the possibility of a global recession affecting the trajectory of blockchain technology. In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, the global economy has taken a significant hit, leading to fears of a recession. This could lead to a decline in investment in new technologies, including blockchain.

Despite these uncertainties, the future of blockchain technology remains promising.
