Generate a metaphor to explain why it's important to be prepared for a job interview.
Going into a job interview without being prepared is like showing up to a marathon without having trained beforehand. In a marathon, the runner must be in good physical condition, know the course, and have the right gear. Similarly, in a job interview, one should be well-informed about the company, have researched common questions, and present themselves professionally. Like in a marathon, failure to prepare for a job interview could result in a poor performance and a missed opportunity.
