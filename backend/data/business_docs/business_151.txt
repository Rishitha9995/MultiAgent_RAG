10. <b>2007:</b> The launch of the iPhone, and the subsequent rise of mobile devices, leads to a significant increase in mobile commerce.

11. <b>2010:</b> Social media becomes a major factor in driving eCommerce sales, with businesses using platforms to advertise and sell products.
