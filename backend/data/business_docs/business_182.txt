Write a poem about the beauty of the night sky.
Up above in the darkened sky,
The stars twinkle and dance on by.
A canvas painted in shades of black,
With speckles of light, no color it lacks.

The moon rests high, a beacon of light,
A calming presence through the dead of night.
It's glow illuminates, in silver hue,
A mesmerizing sight, forever anew.

Silent whispers of a cool night breeze,
Accompany the rustling of the tall trees.
A symphony of nature, a tune so sweet,
A tranquil serenade, impossible to beat.

As shooting stars blaze across the sky,
A fleeting moment, in the blink of an eye.
Wishes are made, with hope in heart,
As the night sky performs its everlasting art.

The beauty of the night, a sight to behold,
A story of the cosmos, forever untold.
A peaceful escape, of time and space,
The night sky, a boundless and beautiful place.
