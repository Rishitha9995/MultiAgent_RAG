Name 3 benefits of using cloud computing.
1. Flexibility and Scalability: Cloud computing allows businesses to easily scale up or down their IT requirements as per their needs. This makes it easier for companies to support their growth or manage their costs by paying only for what they use.

2. Cost Savings: By using cloud computing, companies can reduce their capital expenditure as they do not have to invest in physical infrastructure such as servers and storage devices. Moreover, cloud providers typically operate on pay-as-you-go models, thereby minimizing the cost of deployment, management, and maintenance of IT systems.

3. Increased Collaboration and Productivity: Cloud computing allows for easier collaboration among employees who can access the same data and applications from anywhere in the world. This enhances productivity as employees can work from anywhere, and teams can collaborate in real time, which can reduce the time taken to complete projects.
