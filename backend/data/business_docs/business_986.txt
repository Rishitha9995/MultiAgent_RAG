Name three pieces of software used for business accounting.
1. QuickBooks: This software is widely used by small and medium-sized businesses for managing their financial operations. It offers features such as invoicing, expense tracking, and reporting.

2. Sage: Sage is another popular accounting software that provides solutions for businesses of different sizes. It helps you keep track of your cash flow, manage your inventory, and generate reports. 

3. Xero: Xero is a cloud-based software used by small businesses to manage their finances. It offers features such as bank reconciliation, invoicing, inventory tracking, and payroll integration.
