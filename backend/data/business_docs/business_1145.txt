List some common applications of machine learning.
Machine learning has a wide range of applications across various industries. Some of the most common applications include:

1. Image and Speech recognition: This is one of the most popular applications of machine learning, which enables machines to interpret and understand human speech and images.

2. Recommender Systems: Machine learning is widely used in recommendation systems of online platforms such as Amazon, Netflix, and YouTube to provide personalized suggestions based on the user's past behavior.

3. Fraud Detection: Machine learning is used to predicting and detecting fraudulent activities in various industries such as banking, insurance, and e-commerce.

4. Medical Diagnosis: Machine learning is playing a significant role in the rapid and accurate diagnosis of diseases by assisting doctors to make data-driven decisions based on the patient’s medical history.

5. Predictive Maintenance: Machine learning is used to predict the likelihood of machinery breakdowns, thereby enabling timely maintenance and reducing downtime in industries such as aviation, manufacturing, and oil and gas.

6. Autonomous vehicles: Machine learning techniques such as deep learning and computer vision are used to train self-driving cars to perform various tasks such as object and pedestrian detection, lane keeping, and route planning.

7. Sentiment Analysis: Machine learning is used to analyze the sentiment of texts on social media, customer reviews, and survey responses to determine overall consumer sentiment.

8. Customer segmentation: Machine learning is used to group customers based on their preferences and behavior, to enable companies to tailor their marketing efforts and increase customer engagement.
