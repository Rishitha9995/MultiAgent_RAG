Policy Statement: Office Attire 

Our organization values professionalism and the appropriate representation of our company culture. As such, all employees are expected to adhere to the following policy regarding office attire during work hours:

- Clothing should be neat, clean, and appropriate for a business environment.
- Attire such as suits, dress pants, dresses, skirts, and blouses are considered appropriate.
- Casual clothing such as t-shirts, shorts, and athletic wear are not permitted.
- Clothing with offensive graphics or text is not allowed.
- Footwear should be appropriate for a professional setting, with closed-toe shoes preferred.
- Any tattoos and piercings should be discreet and not offensive.

In the event of a special occasion or event, the company may issue a temporary relaxed dress code, which will be communicated in advance. Employees who are unsure about the appropriateness of their attire should consult with their supervisor. Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.
