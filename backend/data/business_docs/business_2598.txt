Create a list of questions to ask an interviewer when applying for a media job
Here are ten potential questions an interviewee could ask when applying for a media job:

1. Can you tell me more about the day-to-day responsibilities of this role?
2. How does this position fit into the overall organizational structure of the media company?
3. Can you tell me more about the company culture and work environment?
4. How do you measure success for this position?
5. Could you give me an example of a project or a story that the team recently completed and you are particularly proud of?
6. Can you tell me more about the opportunities for growth and advancement within the company?
7. How does the company stay up to date with the constantly evolving media landscape?
8. How does the company prioritize and approach diversity, equity, and inclusion within its content and its operations?
9. Can you tell me more about the team I will be working with if I get the position?
10. Lastly, What are the next steps in the interview process?
