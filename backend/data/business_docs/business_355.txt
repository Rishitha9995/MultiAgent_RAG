Write an introductory paragraph on the evolution of digital marketing.
Digital marketing has come a long way since its inception in the early 1990s. With the advent of the internet and technological advancements, traditional marketing methods were soon supplemented and in some cases even replaced by digital methods. From basic web pages to dynamic and interactive advertisements, the evolution of digital marketing has been driven by the need to reach consumers in a more efficient and personalized way. Today, digital marketing encompasses a variety of methods, including email, social media, and search engine optimization, providing businesses with more options than ever before to reach their audience.
