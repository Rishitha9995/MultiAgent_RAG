Generate a list of five activities that a family could do together.
1. Have a family game night: Set up a game night where everyone can participate, pop some popcorn, order a pizza, and play board games, cards or interactive games. 
2. Go on a nature walk or hike: Explore the great outdoors together, take a family hike in a nearby park or nature reserve. Make it a fun adventure by creating scavenger hunt list for things to find on the way.
3. Do a family art project: Get creative as a family, gather some art supplies and work on a project together. It could be painting, crafting, sculpting or scrapbooking.
4. Cook a meal together: Plan and cook a meal together as a family. Assign different tasks to each family member, from chopping veggies to setting the table. Enjoy the fruits of your collective labor by sitting down for a family dinner.
5. Have a movie marathon: Pick a theme or series of movies and snuggle up on the couch for a family movie marathon. Make some popcorn, prepare some snacks and enjoy each other's company.
