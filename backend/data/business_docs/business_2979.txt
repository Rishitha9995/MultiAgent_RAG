How does artificial intelligence help detect fraud?
Artificial intelligence (AI) helps detect fraud by using sophisticated methods to analyze data, identify patterns or behavior that may indicate fraudulent activity, and alert the appropriate parties. AI systems can be trained to recognize signs of fraud by using historical transaction data and tracking anomalies that deviate from normal behavior. For example, AI can be used to monitor transactions for abnormal patterns such as the frequency of purchases, geographical location, or the amount of money involved. Since AI systems can process and analyze vast amounts of data quickly and accurately, they can help detect and prevent fraud in real-time, reducing the risk of financial losses for individuals and businesses. Additionally, AI can continue to learn and improve over time, making it more effective in identifying and preventing fraudulent activities.
