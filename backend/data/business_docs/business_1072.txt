Define the term "data science"
Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that combines techniques from computer science, mathematics, and statistics to extract useful insights from data. It involves collecting, cleaning, and analyzing large and complex datasets using various algorithms and models, with the goal of discovering valuable information that can help guide decision-making in a wide variety of domains, including business, healthcare, education, and more. Data Science is a rapidly growing field due to the large amounts of data being generated in today's digital world, and it plays a critical role in helping organizations make data-driven decisions.
