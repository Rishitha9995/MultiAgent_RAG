Come up with three product features that a digital assistant should have.
1. **Voice Recognition and Natural Language Processing:** A digital assistant should have advanced voice recognition technology that can accurately understand a user's voice commands and effectively process them using natural language processing techniques. The assistant should be able to interpret the user's requests, answer their questions and even engage in a conversation with them.

2. **Task Management and Personalization:** A digital assistant should have the ability to manage a user's tasks, schedule appointments, set reminders and provide personalized recommendations based on their preferences and past behaviour. The assistant should be able to integrate with other productivity applications and learn from the user's habits to help them streamline their daily routine.

3. **Proactive Assistance and Context Awareness:** A digital assistant should be proactive in providing assistance, offering suggestions, and alerting the user about important events even before they ask. The assistant should be contextually aware, taking into account the user's location, the time of the day and other relevant factors to provide personalized and timely assistance.
