What is a benefit of sending automated emails?
One of the many benefits of sending automated emails is that it allows for better time management and increased efficiency. Once the email sequences or triggers are set up, the messages are delivered automatically, freeing up time and resources that can be used for other tasks. Automated emails also help ensure consistent and timely communication, ensuring that important information is always delivered on time. This can help improve customer engagement, increase sales, and foster better relationships with clients or subscribers.
