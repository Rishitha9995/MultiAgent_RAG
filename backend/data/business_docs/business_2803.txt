One creative way to use AI in marketing is to leverage machine learning to perform sentiment analysis on customers' interactions with a brand across various platforms. This includes analyzing feedback, social media comments, and voice recordings or call transcripts from customer service interactions. By understanding how people feel about your brand, you can create more personalized and emotionally-engaging marketing campaigns that target customers with the right message at the right time, ultimately boosting brand loyalty, driving more engagement, and encouraging customers to take the desired actions.
