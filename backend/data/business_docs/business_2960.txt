Name three factors that influence a customer's decision to purchase a product.
1. Price: The cost of a product is often one of the most important factors that a customer considers when deciding whether or not to make a purchase.

2. Quality: Customers want to know that they are getting a product that is well-made and will last for a reasonable amount of time. The perceived quality of a product can greatly influence a customer's decision to purchase it.

3. Brand Reputation: A company's reputation can greatly influence a customer's decision to purchase its products. Customers are more likely to buy products from brands that they trust and have a good reputation for quality and customer service.
