Make a list of three psychological techniques used in advertising.
1. Emotional Appeal: Advertisers use emotional appeal to evoke strong feelings such as happiness, nostalgia, and security to create a positive association with their product. For example, a car company may use images of a happy family on a road trip to sell their vehicles.
2. Social Proof: Advertisers use social proof to show that others approve of their product or service. This can be done through customer testimonials, celebrity endorsements, or displaying the number of people who have purchased the product.
3. Repetition: Advertisers use repetition to increase the likelihood that their message will be remembered. By repeating the same message or slogan, advertisers hope to make it familiar and memorable to their audience. For example, a company might repeat a catchy jingle in their radio and TV ads to help customers remember their brand.
