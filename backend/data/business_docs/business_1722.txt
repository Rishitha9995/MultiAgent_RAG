Generate five historically significant people of the 21st century.
1. Elon Musk: An entrepreneur and business magnate, Elon Musk is one of the most influential people of the 21st century. He co-founded PayPal and has founded and led several innovative companies - including SpaceX, Tesla Inc., Neuralink, and The Boring Company.
2. Barack Obama: As the 44th President of the United States, Obama was the first African American to hold the office. He served two terms, from 2009 to 2017, and brought about significant changes in American policies on healthcare, same-sex marriage, and foreign diplomacy.
3. Angela Merkel: One of the most powerful women in the world, Angela Merkel has served as the Chancellor of Germany since 2005. She has been widely recognized for her leadership in the European Union and her efforts to address global challenges such as climate change and the refugee crisis.
4. Malala Yousafzai: A young activist and Nobel Peace Prize laureate from Pakistan, Malala Yousafzai has become an icon for girls’ education and women’s rights. She survived an assassination attempt by the Taliban and has since continued her advocacy for access to education for girls around the world.
5. Mark Zuckerberg: As the founder and chief executive of Facebook, Mark Zuckerberg has changed the way people communicate and interact with each other. The social media platform he created has grown significantly since its launch in 2004 and has played a major role in shaping the digital age.
