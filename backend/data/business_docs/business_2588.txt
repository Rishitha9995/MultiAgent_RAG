Suggest 3 strategies to improve the given company's website traffic.
1. Improve search engine optimization (SEO): This strategy involves optimizing your website's content and structure to rank higher in search engines like Google. This can involve technical improvements, such as improving website speed, as well as creating high-quality, keyword-rich content. You can also improve your website's rankings by earning high-quality backlinks from other authoritative websites.

2. Utilize social media: By creating and maintaining a strong social media presence, you can drive traffic to your e-commerce store. Share links to your products, promotions, and blog posts on your social media channels, and engage with your followers. You can use paid social media advertising to boost your reach and target users likely to be interested in your products.

3. Invest in pay-per-click (PPC) advertising: PPC is a form of online adverting where you pay for each click your ad receives. With services like Google Ads, you can target users searching for specific keywords related to your products or industry. PPC can be an effective way to drive traffic to your website, especially for new e-commerce stores that are still working on building up their organic search rankings.
