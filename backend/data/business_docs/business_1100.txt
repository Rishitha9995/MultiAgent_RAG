Suggest a business idea to earn passive income.
One potential business idea that could generate passive income is investing in real estate properties and renting them out. Property rental can provide a steady stream of monthly income while also allowing for the potential appreciation of the property over time.

Another idea is to create a digital product or course, such as a how-to book or instructional video series, and selling it online. Once the product is created and made available for purchase, it has the potential to generate passive income as customers continue to buy it.

Alternatively, you could set up and manage a niche website or blog that features useful articles, tutorials, and resources related to a particular topic. By attracting visitors to your site and utilizing affiliate marketing or advertising, the website can generate passive income through ad revenue, commissions or other monetization tactics.

It's important to note that while these ideas are passive in nature, they do require some initial effort, investment or planning to set them up for success.
