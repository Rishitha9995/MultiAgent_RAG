Give an example of a dated consumer product that could be improved with artificial intelligence?
One example of a dated consumer product that could be improved with artificial intelligence is the home thermostat. While there are already smart thermostats on the market, older models could greatly benefit from the addition of AI. With AI, the thermostat could learn the temperature preferences of the occupants and automatically adjust to their desired settings. It could also predict temperature changes based on weather forecasts and adjust accordingly, and even determine the most energy-efficient settings to save on energy costs. The integration of AI could greatly enhance the functionality, convenience, and cost-effectiveness of an otherwise simple device.
