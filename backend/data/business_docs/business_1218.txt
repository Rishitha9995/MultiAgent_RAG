Formulate a marketing strategy for a new online shopping website.
A successful marketing strategy for a new online shopping website should include the following steps:

1. Define your target audience: The first step in any marketing strategy is to understand who your target audience is. This includes information like demographics, shopping habits, and interests.

2. Develop a strong brand identity: It is essential to create a compelling brand identity that resonates with your target audience. This includes a clear mission statement, a distinctive visual style, and a consistent brand voice.

3. Build an engaging website: Your website should be easy to navigate, visually appealing, and optimized for mobile devices. Make sure it is user-friendly and provides all the necessary information for customers to make a purchase.

4. Leverage social media: Social media is an effective tool for reaching and engaging with your target audience. Use platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter to showcase your products, share promotions, and interact with your followers.

5. Use content marketing: Creating and sharing valuable, relevant and consistent content can help attract and retain a clearly-defined audience. Blog posts, video tutorials, and user-generated content are all effective ways to drive traffic to your website.

6. Invest in search engine optimization (SEO): Optimizing your website for search engines can help improve your visibility and attract potential customers. Make sure your website is using keywords relevant to your business and target audience.

7. Give incentives: Offering special discounts, loyalty programs, and referral bonuses can help attract and retain customers. Use these incentives to encourage customers to make a purchase and spread the word about your business.

8. Monitor and analyze data: Use tools like Google Analytics to monitor and analyze data related to website traffic, conversions, and customer behavior. This information can help you make informed decisions and adjust your marketing strategy accordingly.

Overall, a successful marketing strategy for a new online shopping website should focus on understanding and engaging with your target audience, building a strong brand identity, and using a mix of online marketing tactics to drive traffic and sales.
