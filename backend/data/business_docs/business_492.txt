Identify two advantages of using online banking.
1. Convenience: One major advantage of using online banking is the convenience it offers. Customers no longer have to wait in line at the physical bank or plan their day around the bank’s operating hours. They can access it on their computers or mobile devices and conduct financial transactions when and where it is suitable for them, be it transferring funds or paying bills.

2. Easy Monitoring: Online banking allows customers to monitor their accounts in real-time. They can view their account balances, transactions history, and keep track of their spending with just a few clicks. This helps customers to better manage their finances and quickly detect any suspicious activity or fraudulent transactions on their accounts.
