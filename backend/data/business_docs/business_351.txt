Given the following instructions, create a plan for completing them.
Step 1: Take a garbage bag and collect all the garbage that needs to be disposed of from the designated areas.
Step 2: Secure the garbage bag tightly so that there is no spillage while taking it out.
Step 3: Take the garbage bag to the designated garbage disposal area or bin and dispose of it properly.
Step 4: Wash or sanitize the hands after disposing of the garbage.
Step 5: Take the shopping list or electronic device with the shopping list saved in it, and proceed to the nearest grocery store or supermarket.
Step 6: While there, pick out the groceries that are on the shopping list and put them in the trolley, cart or basket provided by the store.
Step 7: After ensuring all the needed items have been collected, proceed to the checkout counter and pay for the groceries.
Step 8: Pack the groceries carefully in a grocery bag, ensuring the delicate items are placed at the top to avoid any damages during transportation.
Step 9: Transport the groceries back home.
Step 10: Arrange the groceries in their specific storage areas in the kitchen or pantry, and store perishable items such as fruits and vegetables, dairy, and meat products in the refrigerator.
