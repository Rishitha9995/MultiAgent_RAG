Give an example of how people use technology to get work done.
One example of how people use technology to get work done is through the use of computer software and applications. For instance, many businesses and organizations rely on computer programs like Microsoft Word for word processing, Excel for data analysis and organization, and PowerPoint for presentations. These applications allow individuals to create, edit, and share digital documents and presentations to communicate ideas, manage projects, and complete tasks efficiently. Additionally, collaboration tools such as Google Docs, Slack, and Microsoft Teams allow people to work together in real-time, regardless of their physical location, making it easier to collaborate, share ideas, and get work done.
