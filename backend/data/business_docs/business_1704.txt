Name three inventions that have transformed the way we live today.
Three inventions that have transformed the way we live today are: 

1. The internet: The internet has revolutionized communication, commerce, entertainment, and access to information. It has made it possible for people to connect and collaborate from anywhere in the world and has opened up new opportunities for business, education, and social interaction.

2. Smartphones: Smartphones have put the power of a computer in our pockets, allowing us to stay connected, informed, and entertained no matter where we are. They have also given us access to a wide range of apps and services that make our lives easier, from navigation and online banking to fitness tracking and home automation.

3. Electricity: The discovery and harnessing of electricity has transformed every aspect of our daily lives, from lighting our homes and powering our devices to enabling advances in medicine, transportation, and industry. It has paved the way for countless other innovations and has become an essential part of modern society.
