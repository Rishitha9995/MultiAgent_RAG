Create a list of 10 fun activities for a family reunion.
Here's a list of 10 fun activities for a family reunion:

1. Organize outdoor games such as a three-legged race, egg and spoon race, or a sack race.

2. Plan a barbecue or picnic where everyone can enjoy good food and each other's company.

3. Arrange for a family photoshoot, and capture the happy memories on camera.

4. Set up a talent show where family members can show off their skills or hidden talents.

5. Play board games, card games, or trivia games that everyone can participate in.

6. Have a bonfire and roast marshmallows while telling stories and reminiscing about old times.

7. Go on a nature walk or hike and explore the outdoors together.

8. Do a group art project, such as painting or building something together.

9. Have a movie night, and watch a favorite family film.

10. Plan a scavenger hunt with clues and challenges related to the family's history or inside jokes.
