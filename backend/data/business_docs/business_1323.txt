Identify three elements of a good elevator pitch.
A good elevator pitch should include the following three elements:

1. Concise and focused message: An effective elevator pitch is typically brief, lasting only around 30 seconds to 1 minute. It’s important to communicate your message in a clear and concise manner, that’s easy to understand, and straight to the point.

2. A unique value proposition: Your elevator pitch should articulate what makes you or your business unique, and sets you apart from others. This could include highlighting your competitive advantage, a unique skill set, or a problem-solving approach.

3. A call to action: A successful elevator pitch should end with a clear call to action, encouraging the listener to take the next step. It could be to schedule a meeting, exchange business cards, or share further information about your product or service.
