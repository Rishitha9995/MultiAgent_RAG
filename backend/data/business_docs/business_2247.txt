Generate a list of 5 methods that can be used to reduce deforestation.
1. Promoting Sustainable Forestry: Encouraging responsible forest management practices that support conservation efforts while also maintaining the economic viability of forest products.

2. Expanding Protected Areas: Designating more land as national parks and wildlife reserves to restrict commercial activities such as logging, mining, and agriculture.

3. Supporting Reforestation Projects: Investing in initiatives that restore degraded forests and rehabilitate cleared lands by planting trees and vegetation.

4. Promoting Agroforestry: Supporting farmers to create integrated agricultural systems that combine tree cultivation with crop production, thus reducing the need to clear large areas of forest land for farming.

5. Strengthening Law Enforcement: Implementing stronger policies and stricter penalties to discourage illegal logging and other human activities that contribute to deforestation. Additionally, improving law enforcement and monitoring systems to prevent forest crimes.
