Suggest five questions to ask a job applicant
1. What inspired you to apply for this position, and what about our company appeals to you the most?
2. Could you give an example of how you handled a challenging situation in a previous job and what you learned from it?
3. How do you believe you can contribute to the growth and development of our company?
4. In your own words, how would you describe your work ethic and philosophy towards work?
5. Can you tell me about a time when you demonstrated leadership skills, or successfully worked with a team to achieve a specific goal?
