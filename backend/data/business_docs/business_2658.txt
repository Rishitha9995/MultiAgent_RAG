Brainstorm a list of potential interview questions for a C-level executive
1. Can you tell us about your background and how it has led you to your current position?
2. What are your primary goals and objectives for the company?
3. How do you balance short-term and long-term strategic objectives?
4. How do you handle risk and uncertainty in your decision making?
5. Can you describe your leadership style?
6. How do you foster a positive corporate culture and encourage employee engagement?
7. Can you give an example of a challenging situation you faced and how you overcame it?
8. How do you stay up to date with the latest trends and advancements in your industry?
9. Can you tell us about your successes in increasing revenue and profitability for the company?
10. Can you discuss your role in developing and implementing the company's corporate social responsibility initiatives?
11. How do you prioritize and delegate tasks within your team to ensure maximum efficiency?
12. Can you discuss your approach to managing budgets and financial resources?
13. Can you tell us about a time when you had to make a difficult decision that was unpopular but necessary for the success of the company?
14. How do you handle conflicts and disagreements with other executives and board members?
15. Can you discuss your role in driving innovation and growth within the company?
