Suggest an idea for a new business based on the growth of online sales
One idea for a new business that could take advantage of the growth of online sales is to create a virtual personal shopping and styling service. This business would offer personalized fashion recommendations and styling advice to customers through an online platform. Customers would be able to input their preferences, sizes, and budget into the website or app, and the virtual personal styling service would use AI to curate a selection of clothing items from various online retailers. The customer could then purchase the recommended items through the platform. This business could also offer virtual wardrobe consultations and outfit planning services to help customers make the most of their purchases. With more people shopping online, a virtual personal styling service could be a valuable resource for those looking for a convenient and personalized shopping experience.
