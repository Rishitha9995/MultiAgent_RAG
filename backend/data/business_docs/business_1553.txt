Design a cinema poster for the movie "The Matrix"
To design an attractive and attention-grabbing cinema poster for "The Matrix," consider the following elements:

1. Visuals: The movie deals with virtual reality, so choose an image that represents this concept. The poster could feature a high-contrast image of the main character Neo, portrayed by Keanu Reeves, wearing black clothing and sunglasses, set against a background showcasing a matrix-style digital rain of green symbols on a black backdrop.

2. Title: The title should be prominently displayed using an edgy, futuristic font in a bright, standout color like green or red.

3. Tagline: A tagline that summarizes the movie's premise in one captivating sentence should be included. For example, "Reality is just an illusion" or "Free your mind" would be great options.

4. Credits: Be sure to include the names of the director, main actors, and production company in smaller font size at the bottom of the poster. This could be formatted using the same green color as the title, against a dark background.

5. Layout: Keep the layout simple yet powerful to reflect the movie's theme. The main image and title should be the focus, with other elements placed in a complementary manner without taking away from the overall impact.

Overall, the cinema poster for "The Matrix" should convey the futuristic, sci-fi nature of the movie, with strong visuals and bold text.
