Design an app to help people find a job.
The job search app that we could design would aim to make the process of finding a job easier and more efficient for its users. Below is the plan of the app and its features:

**Interface:**
- The app will have a user-friendly interface, easy to navigate, and aesthetically pleasing.
- The homepage will allow you to log in or register for a new account.
- Once registered and logged in, users can create and edit their profiles, upload resumes, set job preferences, track applications, and search for jobs.

**Search Functionality:**
- Users can search for jobs by title, location, or keywords.
- Advanced search options will allow users to filter by salary, company, experience level, contract type, and other criteria.
- The app will also suggest job listings that match users’ profiles, skills, and preferences.

**Ease of Use:**
- Users can apply for jobs directly through the app with just a few taps.
- The app will save users’ application history and allow them to track the status of their applications.
- Users can also save job listings they are interested in to apply later.

**Networking:**
- Users can connect with other professionals to expand their network and get referrals.
- The app will also allow users to follow companies and get updates on their job openings.

**Additional Features:**
- A blog section will provide career advice, job search tips, and industry updates.
- Users can also opt-in for job alerts and get notified when a job matching their preferences is posted.
- An in-app messenger will allow users to communicate with potential employers.

Overall, the job search app will provide a seamless experience to its users, helping them find and apply to their dream jobs with ease.
