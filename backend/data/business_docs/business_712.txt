What does 'blue-sky thinking' mean?
"Blue-sky thinking" is a term used to describe a type of creative brainstorming and innovative ideation where the goal is to generate fresh, new, and sometimes radical ideas, without being constrained by preconceived notions, current realities, or conventional wisdom. It is often used in business and management contexts to encourage people to think outside the box, to dream big, and to imagine all possibilities. The idea is that by temporarily suspending practical considerations and limitations, people can come up with new and innovative solutions to complex problems.
