Design a mobile app that helps people shop more responsibly
App Name: EthicalCart

**Purpose**: EthicalCart is a mobile app that helps consumers make more responsible and sustainable shopping choices. With this app, users can easily access information about the ethical and environmental impact of the products they want to purchase, allowing them to make informed decisions about what they buy and from where.

**Features**:

1. **Barcode scanner**: Users can scan the barcode on an item to immediately see its ethical rating, as well as additional information about the company's environmental and social impact.

2. **Brand directory**: EthicalCart features a directory of ethical and sustainable brands for users to browse, making it easy to find products that align with their values.

3. **Personalized recommendations**: The app uses data from user's previous purchases and searches to provide personalized product recommendations and suggest alternative, more sustainable options.

4. **Sustainability tracker**: Users can track their own sustainability efforts and see the positive impact their choices are making on the environment over time.

5. **Educational resources**: EthicalCart offers plenty of educational resources for users to learn about sustainability and conscious consumerism, including articles, videos, and podcasts. 

6. **Wishlist and Shopping lists**: Users can create and save wishlists and shopping lists within the app to make sure they stay on track while shopping and continue to make ethical choices.

7. **Sales and Discounts**: The app also features curated sales, discounts and promotions from ethical and sustainable brands, to help users save money while also making responsible choices. 

**Overall**, EthicalCart truly makes it easy for people to shop more responsibly, providing them with all the information and tools they need to make a positive impact on the world with every purchase.
