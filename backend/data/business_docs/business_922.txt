Identify the contextual meaning of the word "ecosystem".
Ecosystem generally refers to a complex network or interconnected system that is made up of living organisms and their physical environment, where various natural cycles take place and are sustained by the interactions between the different components. In broader terms, ecosystem can also refer to a community of interconnected entities including businesses, organizations, and services, that create a specific environment and interact to mutually support and benefit from each other. The context in which the word is used will determine its specific meaning.
