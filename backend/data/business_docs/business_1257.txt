Explain what a business plan is
A business plan is a document that presents a comprehensive outline of a new or existing business venture. It's a blueprint that covers the organizational structure, product or service offerings, target market analysis, financial projections, marketing and sales strategies, and operational plans. Essentially, a business plan lays out the mission, vision, and goals for a business and provides a detailed roadmap on how to achieve them. It is often used to attract investment or secure loans and can serve as a guiding tool for business owners and managers to monitor their progress and make informed decisions.
