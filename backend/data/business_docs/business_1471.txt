Come up with an argument that supports the statement that technology has made the world more connected.
Technology has made the world more connected in many ways, providing individuals with fast and efficient methods of communicating and accessing information. Advancements in transportation technology, from airplanes to high-speed trains, have enabled people to travel around the world quickly and easily, physically connecting different cultures and economies. The introduction of the internet and mobile devices has facilitated instant communication and access to information regardless of physical location, allowing people to connect with others across the globe at the touch of a button. Social media platforms and video conferencing software have made it easier for people to maintain relationships and conduct business with others, no matter where they are. Furthermore, technology has made it possible for news and events to be disseminated globally in real-time, keeping people informed and connected to the world around them. Overall, technology has created a highly interconnected global community, where information, ideas, and people can easily flow across borders.
