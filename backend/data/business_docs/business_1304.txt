Compare the products of the two given companies.
Apple and Samsung are two of the largest and most well-known consumer electronics companies in the world. They both offer a wide range of products and have been in constant competition with each other. Here is a comparison of the products offered by the two companies:

Smartphones: Samsung's Galaxy line of smartphones competes directly with Apple's iPhone. Samsung offers a wider range of smartphone options, with numerous models across the budget, mid-range, and high-end segments. On the other hand, Apple provides a more focused range of iPhones with a limited range of price points.

Tablets: On the tablet front, Apple's iPad is the leader of the market. Samsung also offers a range of tablets, but they are not as popular as the iPad. Apple's iPadOS is seen as a more refined tablet experience when compared to Samsung's Android-based tablets.

Laptops: Apple's MacBook line is very popular among users looking for a high-end laptop with a sleek design and optimized performance. Samsung also offers laptops, but they are not as popular as MacBook's. Apple's macOS is seen as a more refined laptop experience compared to Samsung's Windows-based laptops.

Wearables: Apple has been very successful with its Apple Watch, which is the market leader in smartwatches. Samsung also offers smartwatches, but they haven't been able to match the popularity of the Apple Watch. Both companies also offer wireless earbuds, with Apple's AirPods being one of the most popular options, and Samsung's Galaxy Buds line being a popular Android alternative.

Home devices: Samsung has a wide range of home devices, including smart TVs, refrigerators, and washing machines. Apple, on the other hand, focuses only on home entertainment with its Apple TV and HomePod speaker.

Overall, both companies offer a wide range of excellent products with their own unique features and strengths. It ultimately depends on the consumer's personal preferences, budget, and needs when it comes to choosing between products from these two companies.
