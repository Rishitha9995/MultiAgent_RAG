Suggest a holiday destination for a couple.
One beautiful destination for a romantic holiday for couples could be Santorini, Greece. This picturesque island, with its stunning white-washed buildings, crystal-clear waters, and iconic blue domes, is the perfect place to relax and enjoy each other's company. You can enjoy the spectacular sunsets, swim in the warm Aegean Sea, stroll through charming villages, and indulge in delicious Greek cuisine. It's the perfect place for a blissful and unforgettable romantic getaway.
