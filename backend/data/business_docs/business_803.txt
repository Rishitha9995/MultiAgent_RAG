Design a survey to measure customer satisfaction with a new online store.
Thank you for asking me to design a customer satisfaction survey for your new online store. Below is a survey that can be used to gather valuable feedback from your customers:

1. How did you first hear about our online store?
- Friend or family member
- Search engine
- Social media
- Email marketing
- Other (please specify) 

2. On a scale of 1 to 10, how satisfied are you with your overall shopping experience at our online store?
(1 being not at all satisfied and 10 being extremely satisfied)

3. How easy was it for you to find the products you were looking for on our website?
- Very easy
- Somewhat easy
- Neutral
- Somewhat difficult
- Very difficult 

4. Were our product descriptions and images accurate and helpful?
- Yes
- Somewhat
- No

5. On a scale of 1 to 10, how satisfied are you with the quality of the products you purchased from our online store?
(1 being not at all satisfied and 10 being extremely satisfied)

6. How satisfied are you with the shipping and delivery of your order?
- Very satisfied
- Somewhat satisfied
- Neutral
- Somewhat dissatisfied
- Very dissatisfied 

7. Did you need to contact our customer service team for any reason? 
- Yes
- No

(If yes) How satisfied were you with the response and resolution provided by our customer service team?
- Very satisfied
- Somewhat satisfied
- Neutral
- Somewhat dissatisfied
- Very dissatisfied 

8. Would you recommend our online store to a friend or family member?
- Definitely
- Probably
- Unsure
- Probably not
- Definitely not 

9. Is there anything we could have done to improve your shopping experience with us? (optional open-ended question)

Thank you for taking the time to complete our survey. Your feedback is valuable to us and helps us to improve our services and offerings.
