Write a paragraph about robots in India.
Robots in India are being increasingly adopted across various industries for automation and cost-effectiveness. India has a large and growing robotics industry, with many startups and established companies developing and deploying robots for applications such as industrial automation, agriculture, healthcare, and service industries. Additionally, educational institutions in India are incorporating robotics related courses and programs to equip the younger generation with skills and knowledge for a future with increased automation. The Indian Government has also launched initiatives, such as the 'Make in India' campaign, to encourage the manufacturing and innovation of robotic technology within the country. Overall, the use of robotics in India is on an upward trend, with significant potential for growth and innovation.
