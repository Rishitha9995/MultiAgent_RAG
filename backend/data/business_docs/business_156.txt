Suggest 4 ideas that an organisation could utilize to promote digital literacy.
1. Offering Training Programs and Workshops: An organization could offer regular training programs or workshops to their staff, employees or the general public that focus on digital skills and literacy. These programs could range from basic computer skills to more advanced topics such as internet safety, data analysis or social media marketing.

2. Develop Online Resources: The organization could develop or curate a collection of online resources that focus on digital literacy. This information could be presented in a variety of formats, such as videos, interactive tutorials, or downloadable guides, and could be made available to anyone who wishes to learn more.

3. Partnering with Local Schools and Universities: The organization could form partnerships with local schools and universities to help promote digital literacy among students. This could include organizing guest lectures, providing access to digital resources, or setting up mentorship programs between students and professionals.

4. Host a Digital Literacy Campaign: The organization could organize and run a digital literacy campaign, aimed at raising awareness and encouraging the public to improve their digital skills. This could include organizing events, creating promotional materials, and using social media to spread the message.
