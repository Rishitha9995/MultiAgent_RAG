Suggest a plan of action to handle a customer complaint about a defective product.
Here is a suggested plan of action for handling a customer complaint about a defective product:

1. Listen attentively: The first step is to actively listen to the customer's complaint, allowing them to express their frustration and disappointment, and taking note of the details of the issue.

2. Empathize and apologize: Once the customer has explained the issue, it's important to apologize and show empathy for their situation. This can help to diffuse any anger or frustration they may be feeling, and shows that you understand and care about their experience.

3. Gather information: Ask the customer for any relevant information or documentation, such as the date of purchase and proof of purchase, as well as any other details related to the defective product.

4. Offer a solution: Once all the relevant information has been gathered, you should offer a solution to the problem. This could involve replacing the defective product, providing a refund, or offering a repair or exchange. Be sure to explain the process and timeline for the solution and how it will resolve the issue.

5. Follow up: Once the solution has been implemented, it's important to follow up with the customer to ensure that they are satisfied with the outcome. This shows that you value their business and are committed to providing excellent customer service.

6. Record and report: Finally, make sure to record the details of the complaint and the solution provided, and report it to the relevant department for further review and improvement. This can help to prevent similar issues from arising in the future.
