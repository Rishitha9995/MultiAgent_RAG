Explain what blockchain technology is.
Blockchain technology is a decentralized, distributed ledger or database that records and maintains transactions across multiple computers or nodes. It is designed to provide a secure and transparent system for storing and transferring data, ensuring that once data is recorded on the blockchain, it cannot be altered or tampered with.

The core concept behind the blockchain is that it allows multiple parties to share and access data in real-time while maintaining a high level of security and privacy. Each block in a blockchain contains a list of transactions, and once a block is filled, it is verified by a network of computers, encrypted, and added to the chain of blocks. This chain of blocks forms a permanent record of all transactions that have occurred on the network.

One of the most well-known applications of blockchain technology is in the creation of cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin. However, the technology has numerous other applications, including supply chain management, voting systems, and identity verification.

Overall, blockchain technology is a revolutionary innovation that provides a secure and transparent method for recording and verifying transactions on a decentralized network, allowing for greater trust and efficiency in the exchange of data.
