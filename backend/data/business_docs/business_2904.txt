Name five characteristics of good customer service.
1. Empathy and Attentiveness: Good customer service involves listening to customers' needs and concerns, and understanding their perspective, in order to address their issues effectively.

2. Responsiveness: Prompt, efficient and accurate responses to customer inquiries and issues are critical components of good customer service.

3. Clear Communication: Effective communication includes being able to explain solutions to issues in a clear and concise manner, avoiding technical jargon that may confuse the customer.

4. Patience: Customers may be upset, frustrated or confused, and may need more time to understand the solution being offered. Being patient and tolerant during this interaction can make a significant difference in the outcome.

5. Knowledge and Expertise: A good customer service representative should have thorough knowledge of the company's products or services and the ability to resolve a wide array of issues that customers may experience.
