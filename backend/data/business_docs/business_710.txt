Create a list of 5 topics which could be discussed at a board meeting.
1. Business performance and financial reports: The board members would discuss the progress of the company's revenue growth, expenses, and earnings per share.

2. Company strategy and objectives: Reviewing the company's long-term strategic plan and outlining the objectives and targets for the upcoming year or quarter.

3. Legal and regulatory compliance: This topic would address the company's compliance with the local, regional, or national laws and regulations relevant to the company's industry.

4. Governance and management: This discussion would involve the evaluation of the effectiveness of the company's organizational structure, examining the management team's performance, and conducting a risk management analysis.

5. Human resources: The board members would discuss issues related to employee retention, development and training, compensation and benefits, and diversity and inclusion within the company workforce.
