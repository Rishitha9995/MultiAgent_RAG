Tesla, Inc. is a company that designs, manufactures, and sells high-performance electric cars, solar products, and energy storage systems. The company's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. Tesla's vehicles are produced at their factory in Fremont, California, and Gigafactories around the world. In addition to automotive products, Tesla also produces battery energy storage from home to grid-scale and designs and installs solar PV systems.
