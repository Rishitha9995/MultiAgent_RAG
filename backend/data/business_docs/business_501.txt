List two ways companies can manage their online reputation.
1. Monitor and Respond to Online Reviews: One way companies can manage their online reputation is by monitoring online reviews and responding to both positive and negative feedback in a timely and professional manner. This shows that the company values customer feedback and is willing to address any concerns or issues that arise.

2. Engage in Social Media: Another way companies can manage their online reputation is by actively engaging on social media platforms. This involves regularly posting updates, responding to comments and messages, and addressing any concerns or complaints in a transparent and proactive manner. By being active on social media, companies can showcase their brand personality and build stronger relationships with their customers.
