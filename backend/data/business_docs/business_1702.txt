Describe the personality of a lion.
A lion, often referred to as the "king of the jungle," is typically known for its regal, dominant, and powerful personality traits. They exhibit confidence and courage, and are fiercely protective of their pride or family unit. Lions are known to be territorial animals that will defend their area against other animals or prides that may pose a threat. However, lions are also social creatures that enjoy the company of their pride and have an intricate social hierarchy within the group. Overall, lions can have a combination of personality traits, including strength, courage, confidence, and protectiveness.
