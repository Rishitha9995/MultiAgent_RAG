Come up with a new product idea for the company.
Our new product idea for the company is a "Health-Monitoring Smart Mirror". This mirror can be placed in homes and will serve as a personal health assistant. The mirror is equipped with advanced sensors that can monitor the user's heart rate, weight, temperature, blood pressure, and oxygen levels. It can offer daily health assessments and give personalized recommendations based on the data collected. The mirror will also have an interactive display that can show workout routines and healthy meal plans. Additionally, it can track the user's progress, set health goals, and send reminders to complete daily tasks. The Health-Monitoring Smart Mirror will revolutionize the way people manage their health and wellness from the comfort of their own homes.
