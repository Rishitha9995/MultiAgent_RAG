Identify the main point of the following article:

https://www.bbc.com/news/business-54050430
The main point of the article is that major airlines, including American Airlines and United Airlines, are permanently retiring some of their aircrafts earlier than planned, due to the drop in travel demand caused by the coronavirus pandemic. This measure is projected to save money in the long run, since newer planes are more fuel-efficient and have lower maintenance costs, but it is also prompting major write-offs and job cuts.
