List two benefits of studying English.
1. Improving Communication: English is a widely-spoken language, and is considered to be the language of international communication. Studying English can help you to communicate with people from different countries and cultures, making it easier to build relationships and conduct business with others.

2. Access to Information and Educational Opportunities: English is the primary language for many scientific, academic and business resources. As such, studying English gives you access to a wealth of knowledge and information, making it easier to pursue higher education or career advancement opportunities. Additionally, many prestigious universities and institutions around the world offer courses in English, so proficiency in the language can open up additional educational opportunities.
