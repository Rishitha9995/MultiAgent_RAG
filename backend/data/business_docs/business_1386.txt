Given a development project with a specific scope, list the most critical tasks and subtasks necessary to bring the scope into reality.
The most critical tasks to develop an online platform for tracking the course of coronavirus in the world are as follows:

1. Requirements Gathering and Analysis: 
- Define and document project scope
- Identify and prioritize project requirements
- Determine user needs and identify key features

2. Design and Planning:
- Create site architecture and wireframes
- Design user interface and user experience
- Choose the technology stack
- Develop a project timeline and milestones

3. Development and Implementation:
- Set up the development environment and tools
- Develop the platform's front-end and back-end
- Integrate with relevant data sources and APIs
- Implement data visualization and analytics

4. Testing and Quality Assurance:
- Conduct unit, integration, and system testing
- Perform user acceptance testing (UAT)
- Verify that the platform meets the project requirements
- Fix any issues and bugs

5. Deployment and Maintenance:
- Set up hosting and domains
- Deploy the platform to a live environment
- Monitor performance and troubleshoot issues
- Perform regular maintenance and updates

6. Marketing and User Acquisition:
- Develop a marketing strategy and plan
- Promote the platform through various channels
- Monitor and analyze user engagement and feedback
- Optimize the platform to attract and retain users.
