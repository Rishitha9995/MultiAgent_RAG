Write a synopsis for a movie about a small business trying to survive the impact of the pandemic.
In the heart of a bustling city, a small family-owned business struggles to keep its doors open in the wake of the global pandemic. The business, a cozy cafe run by a father and his two children, had always been a lively hub of the community, but now faces a new reality. With restrictions and lockdowns in place, customers dwindle, and the family is forced to adapt. A story of perseverance and resilience, the film follows their journey as they re-envision their business, shifting to online orders and delivery, fighting to keep their dreams afloat. Along the way, they also confront their own personal struggles, brought on by the pandemic’s impact on their lives. Amidst the chaos and hardship, their love for each other, and their passion for their cafe, remains their driving force, as they navigate this new landscape and learn to survive and thrive in the face of uncertainty.
