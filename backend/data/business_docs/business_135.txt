Generate a business plan for a start-up company.
Executive Summary:

Business Idea:
The business idea for this start-up company is to launch a virtual interior design service that allows users to visualize and plan their home decor through an interactive online platform.

Company Name:
The name of our company will be "VirtuDesigns".

Mission Statement:
Our mission is to make interior design accessible and affordable for everyone, by providing a user-friendly online platform that allows our customers to design and visualize their dream home.

Target Audience:
Our target audience is homeowners and renters, aged between 25-45, who are looking for an affordable and convenient way to decorate their homes.

Services offered:
- Virtual room planning and design
- Customized home decor suggestions
- Online consultation with professional interior designers
- Easy-to-use 3D room visualizer
- Shopping lists and links to purchase suggested items

Revenue Model:
Our revenue will come from the following sources:
1. Commission from decor suppliers for each item purchased through our platform;
2. Subscription fees for premium features such as access to professional interior designers; 
3. Advertising revenue from home decor suppliers.

Marketing Strategy:
Our marketing strategy will focus on social media advertising, targeting our audience through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest. We will also reach out to influencers in the home decor space to promote our services. 

Key Milestones:
1. Launch of website and initial marketing campaign (Month 1)
2. Acquisition of 5000 registered users (Month 6)
3. Break-even point (Month 12)
4. Expansion of services to include physical home decor consultations (Month 18)
5. Launch of a mobile app (Month 24)
