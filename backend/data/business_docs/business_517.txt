Find the best solution for the following problem
The best solution for the small business owner facing rising prices of materials while working with a limited budget can vary depending on the specific circumstances of the business, but some potential solutions that may be useful are:

1. Identify alternative materials: The business owner could look for alternative materials that can serve the same purpose but have lower costs.

2. Negotiate with suppliers: The business owner could try to negotiate better prices or payment terms with current suppliers.

3. Find new suppliers: The business owner could look for new suppliers that offer the same materials at lower prices, or who can offer bulk discounts.

4. Increase price of the product: The business owner could consider raising the price of the new product to cover the increased cost of materials, as long as it remains competitive in the market.

5. Reduce other expenses: The business owner could review and find ways to cut back on other expenses to offset the increased cost of materials.

6. Increase sales: The business owner could focus on increasing sales through marketing efforts or expanding to new markets to increase revenue to cover the increased cost of materials.

7. Crowdfunding or financing: The business owner could consider crowdfunding or seeking financing to obtain the funds necessary to cover the increased cost of materials.

The best solution will vary based on the specific circumstances of the business, and the business owner should carefully evaluate all options before making a decision.
