What is predictive analytics?
Predictive analytics is the process of using data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning techniques to make predictions about future outcomes or events. It involves analyzing historical data to identify patterns and trends, and then using this information to develop a model that can forecast future probabilities. The goal of predictive analytics is to help organizations make data-driven decisions by providing insights into what is likely to happen in the future. Applications of predictive analytics can be found in various industries, including finance, healthcare, marketing, and transportation, among others.
