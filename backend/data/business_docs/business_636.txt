Generate three different solutions to reduce water pollution.
1. Increase regulations and enforcement: Governments should enact stronger laws, regulations and penalties to prevent and reduce water pollution caused by industries and individuals. Agencies should closely monitor water sources and increase enforcement efforts to ensure that all regulations are being followed.

2. Promote green practices: Help educate individuals, businesses and industries on the importance of adopting green practices to reduce their impact on the environment. Promoting water conservation, recycling, reduced use of plastics, and the proper disposal of hazardous waste materials can greatly reduce water pollution.

3. Implement water treatment measures: Both public and private sectors should invest in water treatment technologies such as wastewater treatment plants, filtration systems, rainwater harvesting, and wetland creation. These measures can help to remove contaminants from water and improve overall water quality.
