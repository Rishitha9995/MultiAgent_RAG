Create a list of ten skills a high school science teacher should have.
1. Excellent knowledge and understanding of the subject matter in science
2. Good command of pedagogy and classroom management skills
3. Ability to plan and deliver engaging, interactive and informative lessons
4. Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills
5. Proficiency in using various types of technology and tools in teaching science
6. Strong communication and interpersonal skills for effective student-teacher interaction
7. Capacity to create a conducive learning environment for all types of learners
8. Ability to assess and evaluate student progress and give feedback in a constructive manner
9. Flexibility and adaptability in use of teaching methods to cater to students’ individual needs and pace of learning
10. Continuous learner, keeping up-to-date with advances and developments in their field of science.
