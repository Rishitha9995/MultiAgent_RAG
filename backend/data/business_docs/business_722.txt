What is an ecommerce website?
An ecommerce website is a website that allows businesses, retailers or individuals to sell products and services online to customers. It generally features an online catalog or store, a shopping cart, and a system for making secure payments. Ecommerce websites can vary in complexity, with some merely displaying information about products and contact information, while others offer a full online shopping experience, with seamless ordering, payment, and shipping. The main objective of an ecommerce website is to facilitate the exchange of goods and services over the internet, providing a convenient and accessible way for customers to purchase products from the comfort of their own homes.
