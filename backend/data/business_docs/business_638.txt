Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines using advanced algorithms, such as machine learning and deep learning, to enable them to perform intelligent tasks. These tasks include solving problems, understanding human speech, recognizing images, and making decisions. With AI, machines are designed to learn from experiences, adapt to new data, and perform tasks that would otherwise require human intelligence. AI is used in a wide range of applications, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and gaming.
