Pick out 3 companies from a given list and describe their operations and services in brief.
1. Google: Google is a multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products. Its flagship product is the search engine which dominates the online search market. Apart from this, the company also offers other services such as Google Ads, Google Drive (cloud storage), Chrome browser, Google Maps, and YouTube (video sharing), among others. Google also develops the Android operating system, which is the most popular mobile operating system in the world.

2. Apple: Apple is a multinational technology company that designs, manufactures, and sells innovative consumer electronics and computer software. Its most popular products include the iPhone, iPad, and Mac, while its online services include the iTunes Store, the App Store, and iCloud (cloud storage). Apple is known for its sleek design, intuitive user interface, and robust security features. The company's mission is to develop products that seamlessly integrate hardware, software, and services.

3. Microsoft: Microsoft is a multinational technology company that develops, licenses, and sells an array of software products and services. Its core product, Windows, is the world's most widely used operating system. The company also offers other popular software products such as Microsoft Office (productivity suite), Microsoft Edge (browser), and Microsoft Teams (collaboration platform). Additionally, the company provides cloud-based services through its Azure platform, and devices such as the Surface tablet and the Xbox gaming console.
