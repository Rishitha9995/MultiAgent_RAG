Generate 3-5 questions to use in a survey related to the impacts of technology
1. How do you think technology has improved efficiency and productivity in your daily life or at your workplace?
2. Have you noticed any negative effects of technology on your physical and mental health, if so, can you provide an example?
3. Are there any specific technologies or devices that you believe have had the greatest impact on society, and why?
4. In your opinion, do you think technological advancements have reduced personal interactions among individuals?
5. How do you perceive the impact of technology on the future of job markets and employment opportunities?
