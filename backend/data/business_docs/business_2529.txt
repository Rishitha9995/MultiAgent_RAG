What is the name of the most notorious gangster of all time?
One of the most notorious gangsters of all time is widely considered to be Al Capone, also known as "Scarface," who was an American gangster and businessman who attained notoriety during the Prohibition era as the co-founder and boss of the Chicago Outfit. Capone's activities included smuggling and bootlegging liquor, illegal gambling, and prostitution, among other criminal activities. He was eventually convicted on federal charges of tax evasion and sentenced to 11 years in prison.
