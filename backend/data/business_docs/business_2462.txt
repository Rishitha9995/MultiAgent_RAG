Provide three tips for writing a good cover letter.
1. Personalize it: Customize your cover letter for the specific job and company to which you're applying. Address it to the hiring manager if possible, and mention the company's name and the job title at least once. Show an understanding of the company's mission, and explain why you would be a good fit for both the position and the company culture.

2. Highlight your skills and achievements: Mention your relevant skills and give specific examples of how you have used them to achieve successes in the past. This will help the hiring manager see how your skills could be applied in the new position, and it will help them understand what you might bring to the company.

3. Keep it brief and direct: A cover letter should be no more than one page, with clear and concise paragraphs. Avoid repeating information from your resume, and instead focus on the most important details that you want the hiring manager to know. End your letter with a call to action, such as requesting an interview or stating that you are looking forward to hearing from them.
