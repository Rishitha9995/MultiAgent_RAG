Introducing our newest addition to the toy line- Rex, the Robot Dinosaur! A perfect combination of technology and imagination, Rex is a remote-controlled toy designed to inspire creativity and adventure in children.

Standing at a towering 2 feet tall, Rex is an impressive and lifelike representation of a prehistoric T-Rex, complete with textured, vibrant green scales, sharp white teeth, and glaring yellow eyes. However, he is anything but scary- Rex is equipped with soft, rubber claws and a flexible, swishing tail that's completely safe for kids.

With the aid of an easy-to-use remote control, Rex can walk forward or backward, roar, and even snap his jaws open wide. Kids can take control of Rex and lead him through numerous adventures, or they can sit back and watch as Rex performs a repertoire of pre-programmed actions and movements.

What really sets Rex apart from other toys on the market is his advanced artificial intelligence capabilities. Rex has the ability to "learn" and "grow" as kids play with him. He can recognize and respond to voice commands, follow kids around the room, and even exhibit playful behaviors like wagging his tail.

Rex is perfect for kids of all ages, from toddlers to pre-teens- but we won't be surprised if parents find themselves playing with him too! With his advanced features and durable design, Rex is sure to provide hours of entertainment for the whole family.

Some of the Technical Specifications:

- Dimensions: 24" x 12" x 8"
- Weight: 5lbs
- Materials: Durable Non-Toxic Plastic, Soft Rubber
- Movement: Walking, Roaring, Jaw Snapping, Tail Wagging
- Voice Recognition: Pre-Programmed to Recognize Simple Voice Commands
- Remote Control: Intuitive Controls, Easy to Use
- Batteries: 6 AA Batteries (Included)
