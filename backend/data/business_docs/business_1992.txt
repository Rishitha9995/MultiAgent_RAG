The article discusses Limited Partnerships (LPs), a type of business structure that offers limited liability and tax benefits to its owners. In an LP, there is at least one general partner who is personally responsible for the business's obligations, and several limited partners who have limited involvement and no personal liability. This structure helps reduce the individual partners' risk in the business venture, as any losses are limited to their invested capital.
