Generate 3 marketing ideas to increase engagement on a product's social media account.
1. Social Media Giveaway: Host regular giveaways for your product and incentivize the social media audience to participate by following the account, sharing the post, and tagging their friends in the comments. This would increase engagement, followers and potentially reach a new audience.

2. Interactive Hashtag Campaigns: Create a fun, branded hashtag challenge or contest and encourage your customers to share pictures or videos of them using your product along with the hashtag. This can help generate buzz and user-generated content around your product.

3. Collaborate with Influencers: Collaborating with social media influencers who target the same audience as your product can significantly increase engagement. They can interact with their vast audience by sharing posts, mentioning your product in their stories, and creating content that resonates with their followers to entice them to engage with your social media account.
