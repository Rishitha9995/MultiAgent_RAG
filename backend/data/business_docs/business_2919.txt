Write an introduction to the topic of climate change.
Climate change refers to long-term changes in the Earth's climate, including changes in temperature, precipitation patterns, and extreme weather events. These changes are largely driven by human activities, particularly the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation, which release greenhouse gases into the atmosphere and trap heat, causing the Earth's temperature to rise. This process is commonly known as global warming. As the Earth's climate continues to change, there are significant implications for our environment, economy, and communities, including more frequent and intense heat waves, storms, floods, and droughts. Addressing climate change is an urgent challenge that requires collective action and commitment from governments, businesses, and individuals around the world.
