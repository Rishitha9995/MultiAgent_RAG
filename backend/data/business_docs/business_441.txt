Summarize the benefits of interviewing.
Interviewing has several benefits that can help in the selection and recruitment process. Some of these benefits include:

1. Learning about the candidate: Interviews allow the interviewer to learn more about the candidate, their skills, experience, strengths and weaknesses, and whether or not they would be a good fit for the company.

2. Gauging communication skills: Interviews allow the interviewer to assess the candidate's communication skills, both verbal and non-verbal, which is essential for many roles.

3. Assessing cultural fit: Through interviews, the interviewer can determine if the candidate shares the company's values and would fit well within the company culture.

4. Clarifying information: Interviews provide an opportunity for the interviewer to clarify any information on the candidate's resume or application, and to ask any follow-up questions.

5. Building a relationship: Interviews can help to establish a rapport with the candidate and can set the foundation for a positive working relationship.
