Create a plan for a day trip.
A day trip to Canada can be an exciting adventure. Here is an itinerary that can help you make the most out of your visit:

8:00am - Depart from your hotel/house and make your way to Canada.

10:00am -  Arrive in Canada and make your way to your first stop: The Niagara Falls. The Niagara Falls is one of the most famous natural attractions in Canada, and it's a must-see for any visitor. Take a boat tour to get up close and personal with the falls.

12:00pm - After taking in the stunning views of the Niagara Falls, head to a local restaurant for a delicious lunch. Try some traditional Canadian dishes such as poutine, smoked meat sandwich, or butter tarts.

1:30pm - After lunch, make your way to Toronto, which is about a 90-minute drive from Niagara Falls. Once in Toronto, visit the CN Tower, the tallest building in Canada, and take in the breathtaking views of the city from the observation deck.

3:30pm - After visiting the CN Tower, spend some time exploring Toronto's vibrant neighborhoods. Visit Chinatown, Little Italy, and Kensington Market to experience the city's diverse cultures.

6:00pm - After a day of sightseeing, it's time to relax and have dinner. Head to one of Toronto's many restaurants and enjoy a delicious meal. 

8:00pm - Finish off the day by taking a leisurely walk around the city, and enjoy the nightlife in Toronto.

10:00 pm -  Head back to your hotel/house with memories of an exciting day trip to Canada.
