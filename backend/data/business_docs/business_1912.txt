Create an outline of a social media marketing strategy.
I. Introduction
   A. Definition of social media marketing
   B. Importance of social media marketing
   C. Purpose of the strategy

II. Target audience
   A. Defining the target audience
   B. Understanding the target audience’s needs and behavior
   C. Selecting social media platforms based on target audience

III. Goals and Objectives
   A. Setting clear and measurable goals
   B. Creating objectives that align with the goals

IV. Content Strategy
   A. Creating a content calendar
   B. Types of content (photos, videos, graphics, etc.)
   C. Frequency of posting
   D. Tone and voice of content

V. Engagement Strategy
   A. Responding to comments and messages
   B. Encouraging user-generated content
   C. Running contests and promotions

VI. Influencer Marketing
   A. Identifying and partnering with influencers in the industry
   B. Developing a strategy for influencer collaborations

VII. Analytics and Performance Measurement
   A. Setting up tracking and analytics
   B. Measuring the success of the strategy 
   C. Making data-driven decisions

VIII. Conclusion
   A. Recap of the strategy
   B. Importance of continuous improvement
   C. Next steps
