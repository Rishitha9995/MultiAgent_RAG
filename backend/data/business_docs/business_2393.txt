Suggest five things to do in the following city.
1. Visit the iconic Space Needle: A trip to Seattle would not be complete without visiting this iconic landmark, which offers incredible views of the city's skyline, Puget Sound, and mountains from its observation deck.

2. Explore Pike Place Market: This is one of Seattle's popular tourist attractions, where you can browse through fresh produce, artisanal crafts, and specialty food stalls, and catch the famous fish throwing show.

3. Take a ferry to Bainbridge Island: Take a scenic ferry ride from downtown Seattle to Bainbridge Island, where you can explore the town's quaint boutiques, art galleries, and restaurants, or take a nature walk amidst its beautiful parks and gardens.

4. Tour the Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP): This innovative museum is dedicated to contemporary popular culture, with exhibits on music, movies, and video games, including interactive installations, rare artifacts, and memorabilia.

5. Walk through Chihuly Garden and Glass: This unique exhibit showcases the stunning glass sculptures of artist Dale Chihuly, with both indoor and outdoor displays, including a breathtaking centerpiece in the Glasshouse, and a colorful garden filled with glass flowers and plants.
