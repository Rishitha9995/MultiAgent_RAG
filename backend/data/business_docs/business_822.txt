Design a cloud-based contact management system for 1,000 users.
Our cloud-based contact management system will allow up to 1,000 users to store, organize, and access their contacts from anywhere with an internet connection. Here is an overview of the design and features of the system:

1. **User Registration and Authentication**: Users can register for the system by providing their basic information, including name, email, and a password. There will also be an option to sign in using their existing social media accounts, such as Google or Facebook. Once registered, users will need to authenticate themselves by entering their login credentials.

2. **Contact Storage and Organization**: Users can store their contacts' information, including name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and other relevant details. The system will allow users to organize their contacts into groups or categories, such as "family," "friends," "colleagues," etc. Contacts can also be tagged for easy search and identification.

3. **Contact Import and Export**: Users can import their existing contacts from other sources, such as their phone or email account, using a CSV file. Similarly, they can also export their contacts from the system in a CSV format.

4. **Search and Filter**: Users can easily search for contacts using keywords, such as name or email address. They can also filter their contacts by group or category, making it easier to find specific contacts.

5. **Access Control**: The system will have different levels of access control, allowing users to share their contacts with others. For example, a user can give their assistant access to their contacts, but with limited permissions.

6. **Data Security**: All data will be stored on secure cloud servers, with regular backups to prevent data loss. The system will use encryption to protect sensitive information, such as phone numbers and email addresses.

7. **User Interface**: The system will have an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to add, view, and manage their contacts. It will be accessible from any device with internet access, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

Overall, our cloud-based contact management system will provide a convenient and secure way for up to 1,000 users to manage their contacts. With features such as contact import and export, search and filter, and access control, users will have everything they need to stay organized and connected.
