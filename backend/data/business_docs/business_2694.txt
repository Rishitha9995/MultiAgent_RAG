Come up with 3 potential innovation ideas for a running shoe company
1. Smart Running Shoes with Sensors: Integrate advanced sensors into the soles of the running shoes that can monitor and analyze the runner's performance, including distance, speed, and gait. The data can be synced to the user's smartphone to provide real-time feedback and personalized coaching to improve training and reduce the risk of injuries.

2. Eco-Friendly Running Shoes: Develop a line of eco-friendly running shoes made from sustainable and recycled materials to reduce the company's carbon footprint. The shoes could feature recycled rubber soles, organic cotton uppers, and laces made from recycled plastic bottles, appealing to environmentally-conscious consumers who want to make a difference with their buying decisions.

3. Adaptive Cushioning Technology: Develop a new type of cushioning technology that adapts to the individual runner's foot shape, weight, and running style, providing personalized support and comfort. The shoe could use advanced materials and sensors to dynamically adjust the cushioning level, reducing the impact on the runner's joints and improving overall performance.
