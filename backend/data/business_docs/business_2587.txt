Propose a solution for reducing global warming.
One potential solution for reducing global warming is to decrease our overall carbon footprint by implementing various changes on an individual, community, and international level. Here are some steps that can be taken:

1. Increase Renewable Energy: Global efforts should be made to replace fossil fuels with renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power. Countries should invest in the development and implementation of clean energy technology.

2. Promote Energy Efficiency: Governments should provide incentives for businesses and households to reduce their energy consumption through the use of energy-efficient appliances, insulation, and other measures.

3. Support Sustainable Transportation: Encourage the use of public transportation, carpooling, and electric and hybrid vehicles. This can be done through government policies, subsidies and awareness campaigns.

4. Shift to Sustainable Agriculture: Reduce the usage of synthetic fertilizers and pesticides and promote sustainable agriculture practices such as crop rotation, conservation tillage, and agroforestry.

5. Reduce Deforestation: Preserve and restore forests, as they act as carbon sinks and help in reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Illegal logging and deforestation should be penalized.

6. Increase Carbon Pricing: Implement a carbon tax or cap-and-trade system to put a price on greenhouse gas emissions, creating an economic incentive for companies to reduce their emissions.

7. Encourage Individual Action: On an individual level, people can take small steps like reducing their energy consumption, waste and water usage, eating a plant-based diet, and supporting environmentally-friendly products.

It's important to note that the solution to reduce global warming requires global cooperation and commitment to make significant changes in our lifestyle, economy, and society.
