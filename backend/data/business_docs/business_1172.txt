        2. Variations in solar radiation
        3. Ocean currents

        2. Extreme weather events
        3. Loss of habitats

        1. Food and water security
        2. Health and safety
        3. Displacement and migration

    A. Reducing greenhouse gas emissions
        1. Renewable energy sources
        2. Energy efficiency
        3. Carbon capture and storage
    B. Adapting to changes
        1. Coastal defense and flood protection
        2. Sustainable agriculture and water management
        3. Heatwave and disaster preparedness

    A. Summary of key points
