Is the following statement true or false: Artificial intelligence is able to take over all human jobs.
False. While AI and automation have the ability to take over certain tasks and jobs, there are still many jobs that require human skills such as creativity, empathy, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence. These skills are difficult for AI to replicate, and thus many jobs that require these skills are not able to be taken over entirely by AI. Additionally, even in cases where AI can perform a task well, human oversight and judgement may still be necessary. While AI can have a significant impact on the job market, it is not able to take over all human jobs.
