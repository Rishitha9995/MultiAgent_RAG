Describe what a successful job interview should look like.
A successful job interview should be a well-structured and positive exchange between the interviewer and the candidate. Here are some key points to describe what it should look like:

1. Professionalism: A successful job interview should be conducted in a professional manner, and both the interviewer and the candidate should dress and behave professionally.

2. Punctuality: Both the interviewer and the candidate should arrive on time for the interview, showing respect for each other's time.

3. Preparation: Before the interview, the candidate should research the company, the job requirements, and may also prepare for common interview questions. The interviewer should also be well-prepared, having gone through the candidate's resume and tailored their questions to the candidate's background and experience.

4. Clear communication: During the interview, both the interviewer and the candidate should communicate clearly and honestly about their expectations, qualifications, and any other relevant information. 

5. Good rapport: A successful job interview should have a friendly and positive tone, with the interviewer and the candidate making an effort to establish a good rapport. This can include making eye contact, smiling, and using positive body language.

6. Relevant questions: The interviewer should ask relevant and meaningful questions about the candidate's experience, skills, and knowledge to assess their suitability for the job. The candidate should also have the opportunity to ask questions about the company, the role, and their potential future within the organization.

7. Follow-up: After the interview, it is important for the interviewer to follow up with the candidate to let them know the outcome of their application or to request any additional information if needed. This can help to maintain a positive relationship between the company and the candidate, regardless of whether they are offered the job or not.
