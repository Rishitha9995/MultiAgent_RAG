How can someone save money while on vacation?
Here are a few tips for saving money while on vacation:

1. Set a budget: Plan out all your anticipated expenses before the trip and set a budget for each category such as lodging, travel, food, and entertainment. Stick to this budget while on vacation.

2. Look for deals and discounts: Check for discounted rates and offers while booking flights, accommodations, or tourist attractions.

3. Travel during shoulder season: The shoulder season is the time between peak and off-peak season, when tourist traffic is low, and everything is generally cheaper.

4. Use public transportation: Instead of renting a car or taking taxis, use public transport to travel. It’s usually cheaper and gives you a more up-close view of the city.

5. Cook your meals: Consider staying in accommodations with kitchen facilities, so you can make your meals instead of eating out all the time. 

6. Free entertainment: Many cities offer free walking tours, public concerts or street performances. Do some research before the trip to find free or low-cost activities.

7. Buy souvenirs wisely: Souvenirs can be tempting, but they can also be a major budget-drainer. Buy souvenirs wisely, and avoid touristy gift shops in favor of local markets.

By implementing these tips, you can save money and still have a great time on your vacation.
