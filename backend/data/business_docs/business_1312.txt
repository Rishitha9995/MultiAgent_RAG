Construct a timeline for a new social media launch.
Here is a suggested timeline for a new social media launch:

Pre-launch (T-2 months): 
1. Develop and finalize the branding and design elements for the platform. 
2. Set up the technical infrastructure and test for any bugs or glitches on the app/website. 
3. Identify target audiences and create a marketing plan to reach those audiences.

T-1 month: 
1. Begin implementing marketing plan by creating buzz around the platform on other social media channels, through email campaigns, and via partnerships with influencers or other relevant brands. 
2.Monitor the feedback, suggestion, and reviews regularly to learn and improve.

Launch Day (T): 
1. Officially release the app/website to the public. 
2. Host a virtual launch event or livestream to engage with users and promote the platform. 
3. Encourage users to share their experience and invite their friends to join as well. 
4. Monitor usage and gather feedback from users to continue to improve the platform.

Post-launch (T+1 month):
1. Analyze data from the first month of usage to identify areas of improvement and success.
2. Make necessary updates and changes to the platform based on user feedback and usage patterns. 
3. Continue marketing efforts to attract new users and keep current users engaged.

T+6 months:
1. Evaluate the overall success and growth of the platform. 
2. Develop and implement new features or updates based on user feedback and demand. 
3. Host a virtual event or promotion to celebrate the platform's 6-month anniversary and engage with users.

T+1 year:
1. Celebrate the platform's 1-year anniversary and reflect on its growth and success. 
2. Continue to gather feedback and make updates to keep the platform relevant and engaging for users. 
3. Plan for future growth and expansion opportunities.
