Give me a list of five use cases of natural language processing.
1. Sentiment Analysis: Natural language processing can be used to determine the sentiment or emotional tone behind a text, allowing businesses to monitor customer feedback and react accordingly.

2. Chatbots: Virtual assistants and chatbots use natural language processing to interpret user requests and provide appropriate responses, offering a more human-like conversation experience.

3. Speech Recognition: NLP can be used in speech recognition systems to accurately transcribe spoken words to text, allowing for more accurate voice commands and dictation.

4. Language Translation: NLP can aid in the translation of text or speech from one language to another, making communication easier for people who speak different languages.

5. Information Extraction: NLP can be used to extract relevant information from unstructured text, such as news articles, social media posts, or customer reviews. This can provide valuable insights and help businesses make more informed decisions.
