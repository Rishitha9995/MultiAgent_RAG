Generate a list of all the technologies needed to build an e-commerce website.
Here is a list of technologies that are commonly utilized in the development of an e-commerce website:

1. Website Building Platform and Tools: such as Wordpress, Shopify, Magento, Wix or Squarespace
2. Content Management System: such as Wordpress, WooCommerce, Joomla or Drupal
3. Payment Gateway Integration: such as PayPal, Stripe, Braintree, or Authorize.Net
4. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Software: such as Salesforce, Hubspot or Zoho
5. Database Management System: such as MySQL or SQL Server
6. Programming and Scripting Languages: such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, or Python
7. Web Hosting Services: such as Bluehost, HostGator, or GoDaddy
8. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) Certificate: for online security and encrypting customer data
9. Email Marketing Tools: such as Mailchimp, Constant Contact or ConvertKit
10. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Tools: such as Google Analytics, Ahrefs, or SEMrush
11. Shipping and Tax Calculator Integration: such as UPS or FedEx API integration, TaxJar or Avalara
12. Social Media Integration: for sharing products and promotions on platforms like Facebook, Twitter or Instagram
13. Live Chat and Chatbot Integration: for real time customer support and assistance. 
14. Responsive Design: to ensure proper display across multiple devices and screen sizes.
