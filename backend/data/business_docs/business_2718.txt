Come up with three options for a team building exercise.
1) **Escape Room Challenge:** A fun and interactive team building exercise where members are locked in a themed room and must use their problem-solving and teamwork skills to escape within a set time limit. This promotes collaboration, communication, and trust amongst team members. 

2) **Scavenger Hunt:** Design a scavenger hunt with a series of tasks and challenges that require team members to work together to complete. It’s a great way to enhance team spirit and boost collaboration, plus it’s an enjoyable way to explore new places and bond with colleagues outside the office environment.

3) **Cook-Off Competition:** Organize a cook-off challenge where team members are divided into groups and must work together to create a delicious meal within a set time frame. This exercise stretches team members’ creativity, time-management, and collaboration skills. At the end of the challenge, teams can enjoy their creations together and bond over a shared meal.
