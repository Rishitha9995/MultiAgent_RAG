List five areas of life that technology has impacted.
1. Communication: With the advancement of technology such as smartphones and social media platforms, people are now able to communicate with one another from anywhere in the world in real-time. This has revolutionized the way we interact with each other and stay connected with our family and friends.

2. Healthcare: Technology has made significant improvements in the field of healthcare, with advancements in medical equipment and techniques helping to diagnose and treat illnesses and injuries more efficiently. It has also made it easier for medical professionals to share information and collaborate on patient care.

3. Entertainment: Technology has transformed the entertainment industry, giving us a wide range of options for how we consume media. Thanks to on-demand streaming services, we can now watch movies, TV shows, and listen to music whenever and wherever we want.

4. Education: Technology has also played a huge role in shaping the education system. From online courses to digital textbooks, students can now access a wealth of information from anywhere in the world. Virtual and augmented reality technologies are also being used to provide more immersive learning experiences.

5. Work: Many jobs that used to require manual labor can now be automated, and technology has made it easier for people to work remotely. Collaboration tools and project management software have also made it easier for teams to work together, even when they are not in the same location.
