Design a web page layout for an e-commerce store.
A web page layout for an e-commerce store could involve the following structure:

1. Header: At the top of the page should be the header, featuring the company logo, a search bar for products, and navigation links to different pages of the site such as home, shop, about us and contact us. User account and cart icons should also be included for easy access.

2. Main Banner: Beneath the header could be a large visual banner, displaying promotional images of products or deals. These banners could rotate between different images and promotions.

3. Product Categories: Below the main banner could be a section for displaying the broad product categories the e-commerce store sells. These categories could be displayed as a list of clickable icons, providing a quick way for users to navigate to the specific type of product they're interested in.

4. Featured Products: Below the product categories could be a section displaying a selection of featured products. These products could include new arrivals, top sellers or discounted items.

5. Testimonials: A section for customer testimonials could be included, where satisfied customers could share quotes or reviews about their experience with the e-commerce store.

6. Newsletter Subscription: Adding a newsletter subscription form could be beneficial for growing the store’s email list. This form could include an input field for customers to enter their email address and a submit button.

7. Footer: Finally, at the bottom of the page should be a footer, containing links to the company’s legal information, such as terms of service and privacy policy, links to social media accounts and contact information.

This layout provides a user-friendly structure for customers to navigate the e-commerce store and find the products they're interested in, while also providing space for the store to highlight promotions, featured products and customer testimonials.
