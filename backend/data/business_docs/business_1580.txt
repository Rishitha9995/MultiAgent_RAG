Give two advantages of using AI in healthcare.
1. Increased Accuracy in Diagnosis and Treatment: AI algorithms can quickly and accurately analyze vast amounts of data from medical records, test results, and medical images, and identify patterns and correlations that may be missed by human doctors. This can lead to earlier and more accurate diagnoses, and more effective, personalized treatment plans.

2. Improved Efficiency and Cost-effectiveness: AI can automate many routine administrative tasks, freeing up healthcare professionals to focus on providing high-quality patient care. This can translate into shorter wait times for patients, and a reduction in healthcare costs as the system becomes more efficient. Additionally, AI can help streamline clinical trials and drug development, bringing new treatments to market faster and more cost-effectively.
