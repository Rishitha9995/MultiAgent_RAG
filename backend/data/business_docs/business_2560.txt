Design a dialogue flow for booking a flight ticket using a virtual assistant.
User: Hey!

Virtual Assistant: Hi! How can I assist you today? 

User: I would like to book a flight ticket.

Virtual Assistant: Sure. Could you please tell me your preferred departure and arrival destinations?

User: I want to fly from New York to Los Angeles.

Virtual Assistant: Alright. On which date do you want to depart from New York?

User: On the 20th of next month.

Virtual Assistant: Great! And what date would you like to return from Los Angeles?

User: On the 25th of the same month.

Virtual Assistant: Okay. Would you like a one-way or a round trip ticket? 

User: A round trip ticket.

Virtual Assistant: Would you prefer economy, business, or first class?

User: Economy, please.

Virtual Assistant: Sure, let me search for available flights for you. Would you like any specific airline or the cheapest option?

User: The cheapest option, please.

Virtual Assistant: I found several flights that match your search. The cheapest option is with Delta Airlines, which costs $250 for a round trip. Would you like to book this flight?

User: Yes, please.

Virtual Assistant: Alright. Can you please provide me with your full name, date of birth, email address, and contact number to complete the booking?

User: Sure, my full name is John Smith, my date of birth is January 1, 1990, my email address is johnsmith@email.com, and my contact number is 555-1234.

Virtual Assistant: Thank you. I have booked the flight for you. A confirmation email has been sent to your email address. Is there anything else I can assist you with? 

User: No, that's all. Thank you for your help!

Virtual Assistant: You're welcome! Have a great trip.
