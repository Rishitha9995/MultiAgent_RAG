Give a suggestion for a fun activity that can be done with friends.
One fun activity that you could do with your friends is to organize a game or sports day. You could gather together and play different games or sports, such as volleyball, soccer, or basketball, just to name a few. It's a great way to have fun, bond, and stay active together.
