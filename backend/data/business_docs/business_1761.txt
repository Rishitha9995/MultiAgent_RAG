Create a dress code that would best fit a professional business environment.
A professional business environment typically requires attire that is neat, clean, and conservative. Here is a dress code that would best fit this type of environment:

For Men:
1. Suits and dress pants in darker colors such as navy, black, or dark grey are preferred.
2. Button-up dress shirts, in light colors such as white, light blue, or light grey are appropriate. Shirts should also be tucked in.
3. A conservative tie is recommended.
4. Dress shoes, such as oxfords, in black or brown should be polished.
5. Hair should be neat and well-groomed; facial hair should also be neat and trimmed.

For Women:
1. Business suits or pantsuits, in darker colors such as navy, black, or dark grey, are recommended.
2. Blouses, in light colors such as white or light pink should be well fitted, not revealing or low cut.
3. Skirts and dress pants should be knee-length or longer.
4. Dress shoes, such as pumps, in black or brown are recommended. Heels should be 2 inches or less.
5. Hair should be neat and well-groomed; makeup should be minimal and conservative.

Overall, accessories and jewelry should be limited, and body piercings or visible tattoos should be covered. The goal is to present a professional image to colleagues, clients, and superiors, and this dress code will help in achieving that.
