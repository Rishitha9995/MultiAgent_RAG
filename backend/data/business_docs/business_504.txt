List 5 elements that make up an effective mission statement
1. Purpose: A clear explanation of the company’s purpose and why it exists, providing a foundation for all decision making.

2. Values: A statement of the company's core values and beliefs, which act as guiding principles for its behavior and actions.

3. Goals: A summary of the company's key objectives and goals, providing direction for its efforts and focus its energy.

4. Customer focus: An emphasis on the needs and wants of the company's target customer base, helping to align the company's offerings with the needs of its customers.

5. Inspiration: An inspirational and aspirational message that motivates employees, stakeholders, and customers, positioning the company as a leader in its industry.
