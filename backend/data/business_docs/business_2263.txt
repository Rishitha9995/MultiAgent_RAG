Come up with a marketing strategy to promote a new smartphone.
To effectively promote the new Samsung Galaxy S20 smartphone, our marketing strategy will include the following tactics:

1. Influencer marketing - Partner with popular tech influencers to create and share reviews and unboxing videos on their social media platforms such as YouTube and Instagram. This will generate hype and excitement about the new smartphone.

2. Social media advertising - Launch a targeted advertising campaign on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. These ads will use eye-catching visuals and creative copy to highlight the top features of the Samsung Galaxy S20.

3. In-store promotions - Set up engaging in-store promotions at major retailers to drive engagement and sales. This could include special offers, discounts, and interactive demos that allow customers to experience the Samsung Galaxy S20 first-hand.

4. Email marketing - Leverage our existing email list to send out a series of promotional emails that highlight the key features and benefits of the Samsung Galaxy S20. This will include an exclusive pre-order offer for our most loyal customers.

5. Content marketing - Create and share engaging content on our blog and social media platforms that highlights the unique features of the Samsung Galaxy S20. This could include tutorials, how-to guides, and tips and tricks that showcase the capabilities of the smartphone.

6. Event marketing - Host a series of launch events in major cities to generate buzz and excitement about the Samsung Galaxy S20. These events will include product demos, giveaways, and photo opportunities for attendees to share on social media. 

Overall, our goal is to use a multi-channel approach to reach potential customers and generate excitement about the Samsung Galaxy S20, highlighting its key features and benefits, and ultimately driving sales.
