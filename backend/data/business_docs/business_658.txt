Write a script of a sales call.
**Salesperson**: Good afternoon, this is [Salesperson’s Name] from [Company Name]. Am I speaking with [Customer’s Name]?

**Customer**: Yes, this is [Customer’s Name].

**Salesperson**: Hi [Customer’s Name], I hope you are well. I am calling because I wanted to share some exciting news with you about our newest product that can benefit your business.

**Customer**: Okay, I am listening.

**Salesperson**: Great! Our company has recently launched a new software that can improve the efficiency of your sales team by 30%, allowing them to close more deals, and in turn, increasing your revenue. It is easy to use, and our team can provide training to ensure a seamless transition. 

**Customer**: That sounds interesting. Can you tell me more about it?

**Salesperson**: Of course! What would you like to know?

**Customer**: Well, how is it different from other sales software in the market?

**Salesperson**: Great question! Our software integrates with your existing CRM and uses AI technology to provide real-time suggestions to your sales team on how to engage with potential customers. Additionally, it has a feature that automates follow-ups, freeing up time for your team to focus on closing deals. Plus, our customer support is available 24/7 to help you with any questions or issues that may arise.

**Customer**: That sounds very helpful. How much does it cost?

**Salesperson**: Our pricing is very competitive and depends on the size of your sales team. For a team of up to 10 people, it would cost [Amount] per month. But, since you are an existing customer, I can offer you a 20% discount on the first six months.

**Customer**: That sounds like a good deal. Let me discuss it with my team, and I will get back to you.

**Salesperson**: Absolutely! I completely understand. Would it be helpful if I send you an email with more information about the software and the pricing, so you can share it with your team?

**Customer**: Yes, that would be great.

**Salesperson**: Perfect! I will send that right over. If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to reach out to me. Thank you for your time, and have a great day!

**Customer**: Thank you, you too.

**Salesperson**: Goodbye [Customer’s Name]!
