Write a short story of 100 words or less that includes the words ‘rain’ and ‘umbrella’.
It was pouring rain when Jane stepped outside. She regretted not bringing her umbrella, feeling droplets soaking her clothes. Suddenly, she heard a voice calling her name. She turned to see her neighbor holding out an umbrella with a smile. Inside the cramped space, they couldn't help but laugh at the splashes they made along the way. In those moments, walking in the rain transformed from a dreary experience into something heartwarming. As they parted ways, Jane realized that sometimes, the universe finds its ways to make a rainy day special. All it takes is an umbrella and kind company.
