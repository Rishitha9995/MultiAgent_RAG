Create a definition for the term "affinity marketing."
Affinity marketing is a strategy that involves partnering with other companies or organizations that share a common target audience or customer base with the aim of promoting and selling each other's products or services. Also known as partnership marketing, it involves creating a mutually beneficial relationship where both partners can reach new customers, build brand awareness and loyalty, and increase sales and revenue. Affinity marketing can take a variety of forms, including co-branding, product bundling, and joint promotions, among others.
