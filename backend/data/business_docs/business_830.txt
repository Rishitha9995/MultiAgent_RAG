Make a list of 10 criteria for evaluating a job applicant
1. Relevant education and qualifications: Applicants should hold the necessary degrees and certifications required for the job position.

2. Work experience: Applicants should possess relevant work experience for the role they are applying for.

3. Skills: Applicants must demonstrate superb technical and transferable skills to excel in the position.

4. Professionalism: Applicants should act professionally and uphold the company's standards, values, and ethics.

5. Communication: Applicants should be able to communicate clearly, effectively, and professionally with colleagues, superiors, and clients.

6. Problem solving: Applicants must possess strong critical thinking and problem-solving skills to tackle work-related challenges.

7. Adaptability: Applicants should demonstrate their ability to learn and adapt quickly to new situations.

8. Leadership: Applicants should exhibit excellent leadership qualities and the ability to motivate and manage teams.

9. Reliability: Applicants should be dependable and demonstrate a strong work ethic.

10. Cultural fit: Applicants should align with the company culture and contribute positively to the work environment.
