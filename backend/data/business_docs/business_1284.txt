Compose a list of questions to ask when interviewing a software engineer.
1. What motivated you to pursue a career in software development and engineering?

2. Can you describe a particularly challenging project you've worked on and how you approached it?

3. Can you tell us about your experience with specific programming languages and technologies?

4. How do you approach problem-solving in your work?

5. How do you stay up to date with advancements and trends in software development?

6. What do you consider to be your greatest strengths as a software engineer?

7. Can you give us an example of a difficult bug you had to debug and how you went about it?

8. How do you handle conflicts or differences of opinion within a team?

9. Can you describe a time when you had to make a difficult decision related to a project and how you resolved the issue?

10. How do you handle time management when working on multiple projects or tasks simultaneously?

11. What role do you play in the software development lifecycle?

12. Can you tell us about a time when you had to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders? How did you ensure that they understood the information?

13. How do you incorporate user feedback into the development process?

14. What do you consider to be the most important qualities for success in this field?

15. What do you enjoy most about being a software engineer?
