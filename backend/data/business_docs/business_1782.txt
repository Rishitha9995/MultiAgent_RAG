Suggest three strategies that may help reduce global warming.
1. Switch to renewable energy sources: Fossil fuels are one of the major contributors to global warming. By shifting towards renewable energy sources like solar, wind and hydro power, we can significantly cut down our carbon footprint and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

2. Promoting energy efficiency: Making buildings, vehicles, and businesses more energy-efficient is another way to combat global warming. This includes using energy-efficient appliances, insulating homes and buildings, and encouraging people to conserve energy.

3. Planting more trees: Trees absorb and store carbon dioxide, one of the main greenhouse gases responsible for global warming. Planting more trees can help reduce the amount of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere. This can be done by protecting and conserving forests, and promoting reforestation and afforestation programs.
