Describe a small business that someone is thinking of starting.
A small business that someone may be thinking of starting could be a local artisan bakery that specializes in freshly-baked bread, pastries, and desserts made with high-quality, organic ingredients. The business could operate as a traditional brick-and-mortar store or as a virtual storefront, with an online ordering system and delivery or curbside pickup options. The bakery could offer a unique and rotating selection of baked goods, appealing to a wide range of customers. Additionally, the business could participate in local farmers' markets and community events, building a loyal following by providing exceptional quality products, outstanding customer service, and an authentic, artisanal experience.
