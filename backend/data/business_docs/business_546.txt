Create a taxonomy of all text genres.
A taxonomy of text genres is a system of classification that divides different styles of writing into categories. Below is a possible taxonomy of text genres, though there are many ways to divide and categorize them. 

**Fiction**

- Novel
- Novella
- Short story
- Fantasy
- Science Fiction
- Mystery and Crime
- Romance
- Historical Fiction
- Realistic Fiction
- Young Adult Fiction
- Children’s Fiction

**Nonfiction**

- Biography and Autobiography
- Memoir
- History
- Science and Nature
- Travel literature
- Business and Economics
- Self-help and Personal Development
- Art and Architecture
- Religion and Spirituality
- Psychology and Counseling
- Current Events, Politics and Society

**Drama**

- Tragedy
- Comedy
- History
- Romance
- Satire
- Musical

**Poetry**

- Epic
- Narrative
- Dramatic
- Lyric
- Sonnet
- Haiku
- Free Verse
- Limerick
- Elegy
- Ode
- Ballad

**Media**

- News
- Editorial
- Feature
- Column
- Review
- Sports Writing
- Investigative Journalism
- Opinions and Commentary

**Digital/Internet**

- Blog
- Vlog
- podcast
- Social media post
- email
- Chat message
- Wiki pages
