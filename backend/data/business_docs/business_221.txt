What questions should be asked to evaluate a customer service representative?
To evaluate a customer service representative, some questions that could be asked include:

1. What steps do you take to understand the customer's problems or concerns?
2. How do you respond to a customer who is upset or angry?
3. How do you handle unexpected customer problems or complaints?
4. Can you provide an example of a time when you went above and beyond to resolve a customer's issue?
5. How do you prioritize and manage multiple customer inquiries at once?
6. Can you tell us about a time when you had to make a difficult decision involving a customer?
7. How do you stay up to date on company policies and procedures?
8. How do you communicate with other departments or team members to resolve a customer's issue?
9. Can you give examples of how you have improved the customer experience in your role?
10. How do you measure your success as a customer service representative?
