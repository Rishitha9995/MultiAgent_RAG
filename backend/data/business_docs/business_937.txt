Identify and explain one benefit of cloud computing.
One key benefit of cloud computing is its scalability. This refers to the ability of cloud systems to efficiently handle the increasing volume of work and accommodate growth. With traditional, on-premise systems, businesses may have to invest in expensive hardware upgrades or add more servers to accommodate growth or increased demand. With cloud computing, businesses can easily scale up their computing resources by adding more storage, processing power, or bandwidth as needed, without having to purchase, install, and maintain additional hardware. The result is a more cost-effective and agile approach to IT resource management that allows businesses to grow and adapt quickly to changing needs.
