List three arguments why the world should go green.
1. Environmental sustainability: Going green allows the preservation of the environment by reducing carbon footprints, conserving natural resources, and promoting cleaner air and water. The use of renewable energy sources and the reduction of waste and pollution helps to maintain the delicate balance of ecosystems and ensures they can continue to provide necessary resources for future generations.

2. Economic benefits: Investing in green practices and technologies creates new job opportunities in the growing green economy. In addition, implementing energy-efficient practices and using renewable energy sources can result in significant cost savings for businesses and individuals in the long term.

3. Improved public health: Going green can have a positive impact on public health by reducing pollution and promoting healthier lifestyles. Reduced pollution levels lead to improved air and water quality, which has numerous health benefits such as reducing respiratory and cardiovascular diseases. Additionally, the promotion of green spaces and active transportation, such as walking and cycling, can encourage physical activity and improve overall wellbeing.
