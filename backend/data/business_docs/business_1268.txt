Given a description of a computer system, design a user interface with at least 4 features.
Design of User Interface for a Library Management System: 

1. Search and Cataloging: The system would have a search bar, allowing members to search for books, authors, and other resources. Results could be refined by publication date, language, and other advanced cataloging options. 

2. Book Management: One crucial feature is book management, which includes a list of all books owned or borrowed from the library, their due dates, and information about late fines (if any). The system would allow the user to add, delete, and edit books and publications, as well as view and manage borrower information. 

3. Reservation and Holds: The system also includes a feature for members to reserve a book or place it on hold if it is temporarily unavailable. Members could also create reading lists, doing reservations for multiple items at once.

4. Reporting and Analytics: The system keeps track of the most popular books, their loan histories, and other data. This data is used to create insightful reports for members and librarians alike, helping with inventory management and decision-making at the library.
