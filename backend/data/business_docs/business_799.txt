Name a few core concepts in the given area.
Some core concepts in the field of Financial Inclusion are: 

1. Accessibility - Availability of financial products and services to all segments of the population in a sustainable manner.
2. Affordability - The financial products and services offered should be priced at a affordable in order to cater to the needs of low income population.
3. Suitability - Financial products and services should be tailored and appropriate to the needs of underserved population.
4. Financial Literacy - Providing education and awareness to increase understanding and knowledge of finance and risk management.
5. Financial Protection - Safeguards and regulations that ensure consumers are protected from any exploitation or malpractices from financial service providers.
6. Inclusive Growth - Using financial inclusion as a means of promoting economic growth and reducing economic disparities within the population.
7. Technological innovations - Leveraging technology to overcome barriers to financial inclusion like cost of service delivery, geographic barriers etc.
8. Non-discriminatory access - Ensuring everyone has equal opportunity to access financial service, without discrimination due to factors such as gender, race or social status.
