Come up with five questions to ask my new boss.
1. Can you tell me more about the culture and values of the company?
2. How do you see my role evolving in the next few years within the organization?
3. How do you prefer to communicate with your team members, and how often do you like to have check-ins?
4. What do you see as the biggest challenges facing our team, and how do you envision us working to overcome them?
5. How do you encourage and support the professional growth and development of your team members?
