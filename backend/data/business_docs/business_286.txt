Generate a list of five potential solutions to the given problem.
1. Encouraging the use of Public Transport: Government can improve the public transportation system and motivate people to use it rather than using their own personal vehicles, thus reducing the number of cars on the road.

2. Implement a Carpooling System: Encouraging people to share rides with friends or colleagues can significantly reduce the number of vehicles on the road, easing traffic congestion.

3. Implementing Road Toll System: Charging tolls on highly congested roads during peak hours can discourage drivers from using these routes, thus reducing congestion.

4. Increase Bicycle Infrastructure: Developing safe and efficient bike lanes and promoting cycling as an alternative mode of transportation can reduce the number of cars on the road.

5. Implementing Smart Traffic Management: Adding smart technology to the city’s traffic lights and other road infrastructure systems can help in optimizing the flow of traffic and reducing congestion.
