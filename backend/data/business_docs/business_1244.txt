List three key points about the US economy.
1. The United States has the largest and most technologically advanced economy in the world, with a nominal GDP of more than $21 trillion in 2019, according to the World Bank.
2. The US economy is market-oriented, where private individuals and businesses make most of the decisions, and the federal and state governments buy needed goods and services predominantly in the private marketplace.
3. Key drivers of the US economy include the services sector (which accounts for about 80% of GDP), manufacturing, consumer spending, and international trade, with the country being one of the world's largest importers and exporters.
