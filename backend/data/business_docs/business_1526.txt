Generate a list of 10 educational podcasts for young adults.
1. How to Do Everything – A light-hearted podcast that teaches you how to do things you never thought you needed to know.

2. Stuff You Should Know- This podcast delves into everyday topics and explains the science and history behind them.

3. TED Talks Daily – Provides listeners with new ideas and perspectives on a wide array of thought-provoking topics.

4. Philosophy Bites – A podcast that features interviews with philosophers on bite-sized topics.

5. Radiolab - A podcast weaving stories and science into sound and music-rich documentaries.

6. The Learning Scientists Podcast – Covers effective study and learning strategies based on scientific research.

7. The College Info Geek Podcast – A show that provides tips on studying, career development, and personal finance for college students.

8. Freakonomics Radio - Discover the hidden side of everything with this podcast that applies economic theory to everyday events.

9. Grammar Girl Quick and Dirty Tips for Better Writing – A show that covers writing style, grammar, and punctuation to help improve your writing skills.

10. Hidden Brain- A podcast exploring the unconscious patterns that drive human behavior, shape our choices, and direct our relationships.
