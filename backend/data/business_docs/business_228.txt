What do you think is the biggest challenge facing the hospitality industry right now?
Currently, the biggest challenge facing the hospitality industry is the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Due to travel restrictions, concerns about health and safety, and changes in consumer behavior, the hospitality industry has seen a significant decrease in demand. This has resulted in major losses in revenue for businesses, as well as significant layoffs and furloughs for employees. In order to overcome this challenge, the industry will need to adapt to the new normal by implementing stringent health and safety protocols to reassure guests, while also finding new ways to generate revenue through alternative business models such as virtual events and delivery services.
