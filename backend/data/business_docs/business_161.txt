List 3 possible reasons why the given website is not performing as expected.
1. Poor User Experience: One possible reason why the e-commerce website may not be performing well could be due to poor user experience. This can happen if the website is not easy to navigate, pages take too long to load, or the checkout process is difficult and cumbersome. Customers may leave the site without making a purchase if they find it too difficult to use.

2. Inadequate Marketing: Another reason that the e-commerce store may not be performing as expected could be due to inadequate marketing efforts. If the store is not effectively reaching and engaging its target audience, it may be missing out on potential sales. This can happen if the store is not actively pursuing search engine optimization, utilizing social media, or running paid advertisements.

3. Lack of Trust: Finally, the e-commerce website may not be performing well if customers do not trust the site. If the site does not provide clear information about the company, its products and services, and its security measures, customers may be hesitant to make a purchase. In addition, if the site has a poor reputation or has received negative reviews, customers may be wary of doing business with the site.
