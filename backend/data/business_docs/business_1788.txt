Design a website layout for a travel booking platform.
Here is a suggestion for a website layout for a travel booking platform:

1. Header: The header of the website will display the name and logo of the travel booking platform. On the right side of the header, there will be options for users to log in or sign up.

2. Navigation bar: The navigation bar will be located below the header and will include tabs for flights, hotels, vacation packages, car rentals, cruises, and destination guides.

3. Search bar: Below the navigation bar, there will be a prominent search bar where users can input their travel information and search for available options. The search bar will include fields for origin and destination, departure and return dates, number of travelers, and travel class.

4. Featured destinations: On the home page, there will be a section for featured destinations. This section will display attractive photographs of popular destinations, with a brief description and a button to view more information and book travel.

5. Deals and promotions: Another section on the home page will be dedicated to showcasing current deals and promotions, with a button for users to view them all.

6. Customer reviews: To build trust and credibility with users, the website will have a section for customer reviews, where users can read about the experiences of others who have used the platform to book their travel.

7. Footer: The footer of the website will include links to the company's social media accounts, as well as useful information such as contact details, privacy policy, and terms of use. 

Overall, the website layout will be clean, modern, and user-friendly, with high-quality images and intuitive navigation to make the travel booking process as smooth and easy as possible.
