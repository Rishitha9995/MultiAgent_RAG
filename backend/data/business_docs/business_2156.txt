Explain how will a digital assistant help you on a daily basis.
A digital assistant can help you on a daily basis in a multitude of ways. Some of the ways include:

1. Organization: It can help you organize your life by keeping track of your schedule, appointments, and to-do lists. You can easily set reminders for important events or deadlines, so you never miss a task or an appointment.

2. Time Management: With voice commands or text inputs, you can quickly add, change or check events, helping you be more efficient and avoid wasting time. 

3. Hands-free Control: The digital assistant allows for hands-free control of your devices, so you can easily play music, make phone calls, send messages or even control your smart home devices all without having to use your hands.

4. Information: A digital assistant can help you find information quickly by answering questions, providing directions, or looking up facts. All the information you need is just a voice command away.

5. Personalization: Over time, a digital assistant learns your habits and preferences, and can even make suggestions or recommendations that are tailored to your needs.

Overall, a digital assistant is a valuable tool that can help you streamline your daily routine, stay organized, save time, and make your life easier and more convenient.
