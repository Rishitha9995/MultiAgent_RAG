Explain what is meant by “substitutable technology”.
Substitutable technology refers to a new or alternative technology that can perform the same function as an existing technology, but in a more efficient or cost-effective way. This substitution may result in the replacement of the older technology through a process of technological change. The concept of substitutable technology is particularly relevant in industries that undergo rapid technological advancements, such as information technology, electronics, and energy production. By adopting substitutable technologies, businesses can improve their processes, reduce costs, and gain a competitive advantage.
