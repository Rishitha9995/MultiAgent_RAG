Summarize Thomas Edison's life in three sentences
Thomas Edison (1847-1931) was an American inventor and businessman who is known for his invention of the electric light bulb, phonograph, and motion picture camera. He also played a crucial role in shaping the modern electric utility industry with his development of DC electrical generating, distribution, and lighting systems. Edison holds 1,093 US patents in his name and is regarded as one of America's greatest inventors, whose work has significantly impacted modern life.
