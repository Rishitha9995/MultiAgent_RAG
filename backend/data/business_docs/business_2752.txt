What could be a possible use case for a conversational AI assistant?
One possible use case for a conversational AI assistant is to improve and streamline customer service by providing immediate assistance to customers' inquiries or concerns 24/7. A business can implement a chatbot powered by a conversational AI that can understand customers’ natural language, address common issues, process transactions, or direct more complex issues to a human agent. This not only enhances the customer experience by providing quick and accurate responses but also reduces the workload of the customer service team, allowing them to focus on more complex cases.
