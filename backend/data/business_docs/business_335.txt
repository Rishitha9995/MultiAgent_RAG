Describe the purpose of data mining.
Data mining is the process of discovering useful patterns and relationships in large datasets. The main purpose of data mining is to extract valuable information from data and transform it into an understandable structure for further use. By using various data mining techniques, organizations can make better decisions, increase revenue, reduce costs, and improve customer relationships. Data mining is commonly used in marketing, finance, healthcare, and retail, among other industries, to identify customer behavior, detect fraud, predict future trends, and improve business processes. The insights gained through data mining can help businesses gain a competitive advantage and achieve long-term success.
