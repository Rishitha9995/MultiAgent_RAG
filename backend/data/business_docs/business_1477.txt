Generate a story of two friends meeting a new person
Once upon a time, in a town not far away, two friends named Lily and Sam were walking through the park on a beautiful summer day. They had been friends since they were kids and had shared many adventures together. As they walked, they talked and laughed, enjoying each other's company. 

Suddenly, they heard someone strumming a guitar and singing. They followed the sound and found a young woman sitting on a bench, playing and singing beautifully. She had long blonde hair and dark blue eyes, and she looked up and smiled as Lily and Sam approached.

"Hi there!" Lily said, introducing herself and Sam, "You have a beautiful voice!"

The woman introduced herself as Ava, and the three of them struck up a conversation. Ava told them about how she loved playing music, and about how she had just moved to the town from a different city. Lily and Sam shared stories about their adventures and the town.

As they talked, it became apparent that the three of them had a lot in common; they all loved music, adventure, and nature. Before long, Ava, Lily, and Sam had become fast friends. They made plans to meet up again and explore the town together.

From that point on, the three of them were inseparable. They spent their days exploring the town, trying new things, and making memories. Lily and Sam were grateful to have met Ava, and Ava was glad to have made such good friends in her new town.

As they sat together, watching the sun set over the park, Lily, Sam and Ava all knew that they had made a friend for life. And so, a beautiful friendship was born, all because two friends took a walk in the park on a summer day, and stumbled upon a stranger with a guitar.
