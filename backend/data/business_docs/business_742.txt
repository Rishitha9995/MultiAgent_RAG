Make a timeline of the most impactful events in the given field.
1. 18th century - the term "entrepreneur" was first used by Irish-French economist Richard Cantillon.
2. 1814 - Francis Cabot Lowell spearheaded the Industrial Revolution in the United States, when he opened the first large scale factory in Massachusetts.
3. 1901 - The formation of United States Steel Corporation through the merger of Andrew Carnegie's Carnegie Steel Company with other leading steel and iron companies.
4. 1913 - Henry Ford revolutionized the manufacturing process with the invention of the moving assembly line for the Ford Model T.
5. 1943 - Ingvar Kamprad started Ikea and revolutionized the furniture industry.
6. 1975 - Bill Gates and Paul Allen created Microsoft Corporation and popularized personal computing.
7. 1980 - Multi-Level Marketing (MLM) emerges as one of the fastest-growing trends in the entrepreneurial space.
8. 1994 - Jeff Bezos started Amazon, which went on to transform the retail industry.
9. 1995 - eBay was founded by Pierre Omidyar, revolutionizing the way we buy and sell goods online.
10. 2004 - Mark Zuckerberg founded Facebook, changing the way we connect and interact online.
11. 2007 - Introduction of Apple’s iPhone and the creation of the App Store revolutionized smartphones and mobile app development.
12. 2008 - The first Bitcoin was mined, initiating the era of cryptocurrencies.
13. 2009 - Uber’s launch disrupts the taxi industry and contributes to the growth of the gig economy.
14. 2020 - COVID-19 pandemic brings in new business models surrounding remote work, online shopping, and virtual events.
