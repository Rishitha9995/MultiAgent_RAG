Identify three positive impacts of technology.
1. Enhanced Communication and Connectivity: Technology has transformed the way people communicate, providing various tools that enable faster and easier exchange of information. Aside from personal communication, businesses have also hugely benefited from enhanced connectivity, enabling virtual teamwork, remote working, and worldwide collaboration.

2. Improved Access to Information: The proliferation of smart devices and advancements in the internet have made vast amounts of information accessible in a matter of seconds. People can keep up-to-date with global happenings, educate themselves on new topics, and learn new skills online.

3. Increased Efficiency and Productivity: With technology, manual and repetitive tasks can be automated, allowing for increased efficiency and productivity. Computer software, machinery, and AI-powered tools have radically enhanced the speed, accuracy, and consistency of work, resulting in better results and higher output across various industries.
