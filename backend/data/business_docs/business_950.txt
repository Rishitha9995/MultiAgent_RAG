Identify 5 sources of finance.
1. Personal savings: An individual or business owner may use their own savings to finance their needs, whether it's starting a new business or making an investment.

2. Loans: Borrowing money from a financial institution, such as a bank or credit union, is another common source of finance. Loans can be obtained for various purposes, and the lender will typically charge interest on the amount loaned.

3. Investors: Business owners may also seek funding from investors, who provide capital in exchange for an ownership stake or a share of the profits. This can include venture capitalists, angel investors, and private equity firms.

4. Grants: Government or other organizations may offer grants for specific purposes, such as starting a small business or engaging in research and development. Unlike loans, grants do not have to be repaid.

5. Crowdfunding: Another increasingly popular source of finance is crowdfunding, where individuals and organizations can raise money from a large number of people, typically via the internet. Crowdfunding campaigns can take various forms, including rewards-based crowdfunding or equity crowdfunding.
