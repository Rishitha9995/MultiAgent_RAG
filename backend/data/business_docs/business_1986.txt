Specify a list of online resources to create a budget
1. Mint: An all-in-one money management website and app that helps track income and expenses, and create budgets.

2. EveryDollar: A budgeting website and app created by financial expert, Dave Ramsey.

3. Personal Capital: A money management website and app with tools for tracking spending, creating budgets, and planning for the future.

4. YNAB (You Need A Budget): A budgeting app and website that follows a zero-based budgeting method.

5. Microsoft Excel Budget Templates: Microsoft offers free templates that can be downloaded and customized to create a budget.

6. Google Sheets: Similar to Excel, Google Sheets allows for custom-made budget spreadsheets that can be shared and accessed from anywhere.

7. BudgetSimple: A web-based budgeting tool that provides spending insights and personalized recommendations.

8. PocketGuard: An app and website that tracks your expenses and income and helps create a budget plan based on your spending habits.

9. Buxfer: A personal finance app and website for tracking expenses, creating budgets, and managing money.

10. Tiller Money: A service that automates the updating of spreadsheets for budgeting and tracking finance.
