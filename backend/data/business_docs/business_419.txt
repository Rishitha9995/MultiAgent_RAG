List three fundamental elements required for a successful business plan.
The three fundamental elements required for a successful business plan are:

1. A clear and concise business concept: One of the most important elements of a business plan is a clear, concise description of the business concept, which explains what the business is all about, the products or services offered and the target customers.
 
2. A thorough market analysis: It is essential to conduct thorough market research and analysis to understand the target market, competition, and industry trends. This will help to identify opportunities and threats and develop a strategic plan to position the business for success.

3. A well-defined financial plan: A successful business plan must include a detailed financial plan that outlines the projected revenue, expenses, and profitability of the business. This includes creating realistic financial projections, identifying potential sources of funding and developing a strategy for managing cash flow.
