Proposal for New Project

Executive Summary
We are proposing a new project that focuses on developing a mobile application for our organization. This application aims to provide our customers with more easy and instant access to our products and services. As the world is moving towards a more digital age, where people rely heavily on smartphone technology, there is an urgent need for companies to keep up with this trend by developing new and innovative ways to connect with their customers. By launching a mobile application, we believe that we can increase our customer base, engagement and overall revenue.

Background
In recent years, there has been a significant increase in the use of mobile applications. People are utilizing apps for a variety of purposes ranging from shopping, banking, entertainment, and communication. Given this trend, it has become necessary for businesses to establish an online presence through a mobile application. This not only makes it easier for customers to access their products and services, it also helps companies to keep up with the changing market trends and remain competitive.

Project Objective
The main objective of this project is to develop a mobile application for our organization. The application will serve as a platform for customers to access our products and services, make purchases and engage with our organization. The end goal is to increase our customer base, engagement and overall revenue.

Scope of the Project
The project will involve the development of a mobile application for both IOS and Android platforms. This will require the organization to work with a team of developers to design and develop the app. The project will also involve the integration of the app with our existing systems to ensure a seamless user experience.

The project will be divided into the following phases:
1. Planning and Requirements Gathering
2. Design and Development
3. Testing and Quality Assurance
4. Deployment and Maintenance

Resources Required
The project will require resources in the form of personnel, funds and time. A team of developers with expertise in mobile application development will be required. Additionally, a project manager will be appointed to oversee the project and ensure it stays on track. Funds will also be required to cover the costs of development and deployment.

Timeline
The project is expected to be completed over a period of 6 months. The detailed timeline for each phase of the project is as follows:
1. Planning and Requirements Gathering: 1 month
2. Design and Development: 3 months
3. Testing and Quality Assurance: 1 month
4. Deployment and Maintenance: 1 month
