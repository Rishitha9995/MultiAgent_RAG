Name five ways to reduce usage of single-use plastics.
Here are five ways to reduce usage of single-use plastics:

1. Limiting Plastic Packaging: Opt for products with paper or biodegradable packaging, or buy fresh produce at farmers' markets where there is no packaging involved, is a great way to reduce single-use plastics.

2. Bring Your Own: Carry reusable shopping bags, water bottles, travel mugs, utensils or containers with you wherever you go. This will decrease the need to use single-use plastic alternatives. 

3. Say No to Straws: Say no to plastic straws, an easy and avoidable single-use plastic item, instead use reusable or biodegradable options such as paper or bamboo straws. 

4. Recycle: Dispose of plastics appropriately and make sure to recycle as much as possible to reduce the demand for new plastic production. 

5. Support Businesses Committed to Sustainability: Engage with eco-friendly companies that have made the switch to sustainable and minimal plastic packaging or are focused on reducing their carbon footprint. Simply put, vote with your dollar.
