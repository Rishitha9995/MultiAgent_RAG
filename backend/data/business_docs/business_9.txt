Give a description of the following customer segment.
Online shoppers in the US aged 18-25 represent a young adult, tech-savvy customer segment that frequently engages in e-commerce activities. This age group is likely to be digitally native, having grown up with the internet and being comfortable with technology. They are likely to use various digital platforms and devices to research, compare, and purchase products and services online. They may have different motivations for shopping online, including convenience, variety, and the ability to quickly find and compare the best deals. They may also have high expectations for fast and free shipping, easy returns, and personalized recommendations. This is a valuable customer segment for businesses, as they have a high level of engagement and potential for long-term loyalty.
