Create a list of 5 questions to ask before investing in stocks.
1. What are my investment goals and how does investing in stocks align with these goals?
2. Am I willing to take the risks associated with investing in the stock market?
3. How much capital can I afford to allocate towards investing in stocks without putting my financial stability at risk?
4. How much time am I willing to dedicate towards researching and managing my stock investments?
5. Do I have a well-diversified investment portfolio and am I looking to further diversify by investing in stocks?
