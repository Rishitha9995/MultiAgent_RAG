Suggest two marketing strategies for launching a new app.
1. Utilize social media platforms: A very effective way to market and launch a new app is to take advantage of social media platforms to create a strong buzz around the app. Use targeted ads, influencer outreach, and regular organic posts to generate hype, interest, and followers for the app. Make sure to encourage early adopters to post about their experiences and also to share the app with their followers.

2. Partnership and collaboration: Partnering with an established brand or company that is aligned with your target demographic can be a powerful launch strategy. Collaborations can lead to increased exposure and credibility, allowing you to reach an already-established audience. For instance, you could offer exclusive features or perks to the customers of the partnered brand, or even create a co-branded version of the app. This can help generate excitement and interest, quickly growing your user base.
