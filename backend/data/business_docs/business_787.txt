Define the term urban renewal.
Urban renewal is the process of redeveloping and revitalizing deteriorated or underused areas in cities. This process involves rebuilding or renovating residential, commercial, and industrial structures or infrastructure in urban areas. Urban renewal is often undertaken with the goal of improving the livability and economic viability of a neighborhood or city, and may involve the relocation of residents, businesses, and community organizations. Projects can range from small-scale improvements to the demolition and reconstruction of entire neighborhoods. Urban renewal is a complex and sometimes controversial process that involves city planners, government officials, developers, and community members working together to create positive change in their cities.
