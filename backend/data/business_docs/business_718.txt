Describe 3 benefits of effective time management.
1. Increased Productivity: Effective time management allows individuals to prioritize important and urgent tasks effectively, minimizing the time spent on unnecessary activities. This results in increased productivity and efficiency, allowing individuals to accomplish more in a shorter period.

2. Reduced Stress: By planning and organizing tasks in advance, people can avoid feeling overwhelmed and stressed due to a large amount of work. Effective time management also involves allocating time for resting and self-care, which further helps to reduce stress and maintain mental and physical wellbeing.

3. More Opportunities: Effective time management enables individuals to finish their work in a timely manner, freeing up time for more opportunities. This can range from undertaking new projects, acquiring new skills, spending more time with family and friends, or engaging in leisure activities. As a result, individuals can strike a better work-life balance and experience a greater sense of fulfillment.
