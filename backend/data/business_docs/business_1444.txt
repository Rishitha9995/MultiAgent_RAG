Summarize the main points of the article "How to write an effective resume".
The article "How to write an effective resume" provides tips for creating a resume that stands out and showcases your skills and experiences. Here are the main points from the article:
1. Tailor your resume to the job you are applying for by carefully reading the job description and including relevant keywords.
2. Use a clear and easy-to-read format, with headings, bullet points, and consistent font style and size.
3. Start with a strong summary or objective section that highlights your most relevant skills and experiences, and what you can bring to the company.
4. Include your work experience in reverse chronological order, focusing on your achievements and accomplishments rather than just listing your duties.
5. Include any relevant education, certifications, and training.
6. Keep it concise, ideally one or two pages, and use active language.
7. Proofread several times for spelling, grammar, and formatting errors.
The article emphasizes the importance of presenting yourself in the best way possible by highlighting your skills and experiences, while also being concise and easy to read.
